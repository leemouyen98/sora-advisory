#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases 

Have you ever had, been diagnosed to have, been investigated or treated for or advised to seek any medical or surgical treatment for any of the conditions listed below :

Disorder or disease of muscle, bones, limbs, spine, back or joints, deformity, amputation, backache, slipped disc, spondylosis, prolapsed intervertebral disc, arthritis, osteoporosis, rheumatism, bone fracture, back trouble or other musculoskeletal disorders ?

# DISEASE 疾病

Is an illness or sickness characterised by specific signs or symptoms.

There are four main types of disease : infectious diseases, deficiency diseases, hereditary diseases, and physiological diseases.

# DISORDERS 障碍 / 紊乱 / 毛病

A disturbance of normal functioning of the mind or body. Disorders may be caused by genetic factors, disease, or trauma.

A disorder could be defined as a set of problems, which result in causing significant difficulty, distress, impairment and/or suffering in a person’s daily life.

# Deformity 畸形

Alteration in or distortion of the natural form of a part, organ, or the entire body. It may be acquired or congenital. If present after injury, deformity usually implies the presence of bone fracture, bone dislocation, or both.

# Amputation 截肢术

Is the removal of a limb by trauma, medical illness, or surgery. As a surgical measure, it is used to control pain or a disease process in the affected limb, such as malignancy or gangrene. In some cases, it is carried out on individuals as a preventive surgery for such problems.

# Bones 骨头

Bones of all shapes and sizes support your body, protect organs and tissues, store calcium and fat and produce blood cells. A bone’s hard outside shell surrounds a spongy center. Bones provide structure and form for your body.

![[Bones 01.png]]

# Muscles 肌肉

Muscles, attached to bones or internal organs and blood vessels, are responsible for movement. Nearly all movement in the body is the result of muscle contraction.

![[Muscles 01.png]]

# Joints 关节

Joints are the areas where 2 or more bones meet. Most joints are mobile, allowing the bones to move. Joints consist of the following: Cartilage. Cartilage helps reduce the friction of movement within a joint.

![[Joints 01.png]]

# Limbs 四肢

In the human body, arms and legs are commonly called upper limbs and lower limbs, respectively. Arms are connected to the torso or trunk at the shoulder and legs are connected at the hip girdles.

![[Limbs 01.png]]

![[Limbs 02.png]]

# Spine 脊柱

The column of bone known as the vertebral column, which surrounds and protects the spinal cord. The spine can be categorized according to level of the body: i.e., cervical spine (neck), thoracic spine (upper and middle back), and lumbar spine (lower back).

![[Spine 01.png]]

# Connective Tissue 结缔组织

Tissue that supports, protects, and gives structure to other tissues and organs in the body. Connective tissue also stores fat, helps move nutrients and other substances between tissues and organs, and helps repair damaged tissue.

Connective tissue, group of tissues in the body that maintain the form of the body and its organs and provide cohesion and internal support.

![[Connective Tissue 01.png]]

# Immune System 免疫系统

The organs and processes of the body that provide resistance to infection and toxins. Organs include the thymus, bone marrow, and lymph nodes. The immune system is a vast and complex interconnected network of many different organs, cells and proteins that work together to protect the body from illness.
