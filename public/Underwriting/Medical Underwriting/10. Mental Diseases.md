Have you ever had, been diagnosed to have, been investigated or treated for or advised to seek any medical or surgical treatment for any of the conditions listed below :

Depression, anxiety, schizophrenia, suicide attempt, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), autism, Down’s syndrome, dementia, or any other mental health or psychiatric illness ?

# Disease 疾病

Is an illness or sickness characterised by specific signs or symptoms.

There are four main types of disease : infectious diseases, deficiency diseases, hereditary diseases, and physiological diseases.

# Disorders 障碍 / 紊乱 / 毛病

A disturbance of normal functioning of the mind or body. Disorders may be caused by genetic factors, disease, or trauma.

A disorder could be defined as a set of problems, which result in causing significant difficulty, distress, impairment and/or suffering in a person’s daily life.

# Psychiatric Illness 精神疾病

Also called mental health disorders, refers to a wide range of mental health conditions — disorders that affect your mood, thinking and behavior. Examples of mental illness include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, eating disorders and addictive behaviors.

Causes can be due to childhood abuse, trauma, or neglect. social isolation or loneliness. experiencing discrimination and stigma, including racism. social disadvantage, poverty or debt.
