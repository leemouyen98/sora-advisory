#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Is a hard deposit of minerals, acid and salt that clump together in the urine. Normally, the stones are small and pass unnoticed through the urinary tract. However, some stones become large, causing them to block the pathways where urine needs to travel.

Concentrated urine can crystallize and form stones. Some infections can lead to bladder stones. Sometimes an underlying condition that affects the bladder’s ability to hold, store or eliminate urine can result in bladder stone formation.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such CT scan / MRI, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如CT扫描/MRI、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any surgery has been performed ? If yes, please state the date & type of operation.
> 3) 是否进行过任何手术？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 5) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 5) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine FEME / scan test ?
> a) 任何相关报告，如 blood / urine FEME / scan test 。
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed.
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告。
>
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor
> 2) 体格检查 / 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果可能性

> Life / TPD / CI:
> 人寿保险 / 全残保险 / 严重疾病保险：
> – Standard case if no complications
> – 无并发症者，标准承保
>
> Medical insurance:
> 医疗保险：
> – Standard case for single episode, no recurrence for more than 2 years
> – 单次发作且2年以上无复发者，标准承保
> – Otherwise; Exclusion
> – 否则；除外承保
