#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Urinary system stones are hard deposits of minerals, acids, and salts that form in the urine and may lodge anywhere in the urinary tract. Small stones often pass unnoticed, but larger ones can block urine flow and cause significant pain and complications. Concentrated urine, certain infections, and conditions affecting bladder function can all contribute to stone formation.
泌尿系统结石是由矿物质、酸和盐分在尿液中聚集形成的坚硬沉积物，可积聚于泌尿系统任何部位。小结石往往可自行排出而不引起注意，但较大的结石可能堵塞尿流，导致剧烈疼痛和并发症。尿液浓缩、某些感染以及影响膀胱功能的疾病，均可促使结石形成。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT scan / MRI, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如CT扫描/MRI、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any surgery performed? If yes, please state the date & type of operation.
> 3) 是否进行过手术？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 4) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 5) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 5) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine FEME（尿液常规）& scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：血液、尿液常规检查及扫描检查
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行手术，请附上手术/切除报告
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病：
> - Standard if no complications
> - 无并发症者，标准承保
>
> Medical:
> 医疗：
> - Standard for single episode with no recurrence for more than 2 years
> - 单次发作且2年以上无复发者，标准承保
> - Otherwise — Exclusion
> - 否则 — 除外承保
