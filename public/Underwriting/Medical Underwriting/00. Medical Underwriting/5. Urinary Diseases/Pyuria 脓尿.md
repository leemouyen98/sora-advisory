#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Pyuria is the presence of pus (white blood cells) in the urine, making it appear cloudy and foul-smelling. It is defined as more than 10 white blood cells per high-power field in centrifuged urine. The most common cause is a bacterial urinary tract infection, but it may also result from sexually transmitted infections, viral infections, or prolonged use of certain medications.
脓尿是指尿液中含有脓液（白血球），导致尿液浑浊并伴有异味，定义为离心尿液在高倍视野下白血球超过10个。最常见的原因是细菌性尿路感染，也可能由性传播感染、病毒感染或长期使用某些药物引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## 所需文件
