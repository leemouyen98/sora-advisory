#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Hydronephrosis occurs when one or both kidneys become swollen due to urine backing up inside them — usually because of a blockage in the urinary tract. It can affect any age group, including unborn babies detected during prenatal scans. Most people recover well with prompt treatment, but if left untreated, severe cases can damage the kidneys and lead to kidney failure.
肾积水是指一侧或双侧肾脏因尿液积聚而肿大，通常由泌尿道受阻所致。任何年龄均可发生，包括在产前超声检查中发现的胎儿。及时治疗后大多数患者可完全康复，但若不及时处理，严重情况可能损伤肾脏并导致肾衰竭。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood (renal function test) & urine FEME? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、肾功能血液检查及尿液常规检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood（肾功能检查）, urine FEME（尿液常规）& ultrasound scan
> a) 最新相关报告：肾功能血液检查、尿液常规检查及超声波扫描
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
