#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Cystitis is inflammation of the urinary bladder, most commonly caused by a bacterial infection. It causes a frequent, urgent need to urinate along with a burning sensation when passing urine — blood in the urine may also occur. Antibiotics are the standard first-line treatment.
膀胱炎是膀胱的炎症，最常见的原因是细菌感染。患者通常会出现尿频、尿急及排尿时有灼热感等症状，有时还伴有血尿。抗生素是治疗细菌性膀胱炎的标准首选疗法。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：血液、尿液及扫描检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with cystitis on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Treated with a short course of antibiotics, fully recovered with no complications.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有膀胱炎，接受短期抗生素治疗后完全康复，无并发症。

> dd/mm/yy 完成的扫描、血液及尿液检查报告随附。

> 自此已完全康复。
