#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Is a buildup of toxins in your blood. It occurs when the kidneys stop filtering toxins out through your urine. These waste products are normally excreted by the kidneys in urine.

Uremia is often a sign of end-stage renal (kidney) disease.  

Treatments include medication, dialysis and kidney transplant surgery.

![[Uraemia 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such scanning, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.  
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 5) Fully recovered ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor  
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> 
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor
 
## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have uraemia on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Blood & urine test done on dd/mm/yy is attached.  
> On regular medical follow up
