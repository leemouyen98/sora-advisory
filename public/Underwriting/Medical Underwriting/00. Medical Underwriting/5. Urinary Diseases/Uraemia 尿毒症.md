#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Uraemia is a dangerous build-up of toxins and waste products in the bloodstream that occurs when the kidneys can no longer filter them out effectively. It is typically a sign of end-stage renal (kidney) disease. Symptoms include fatigue, nausea, confusion, and fluid retention. Treatment options include dialysis or kidney transplant surgery.
尿毒症是指当肾脏无法有效过滤废物时，毒素和废物在血液中大量积聚的危险状态，通常是终末期肾病的标志性表现。患者可出现疲倦、恶心、意识模糊及体液滞留等症状，治疗方式包括透析或肾脏移植手术。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
