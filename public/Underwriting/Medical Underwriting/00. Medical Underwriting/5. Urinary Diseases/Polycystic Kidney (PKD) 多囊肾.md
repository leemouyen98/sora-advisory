#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Polycystic kidney disease (PKD) is a genetic disorder where numerous fluid-filled cysts grow in the kidneys over time. Unlike simple kidney cysts, PKD cysts can significantly change the shape and size of the kidneys. As cysts multiply and enlarge, they progressively replace healthy kidney tissue, reducing kidney function and potentially leading to kidney failure. PKD is caused by a gene mutation, usually inherited from a parent.
多囊肾病（PKD）是一种遗传性疾病，肾脏内会陆续长出大量充满液体的囊肿。与良性的单纯性肾囊肿不同，多囊肾的囊肿会显著改变肾脏的形态和大小。随着囊肿不断增多和增大，正常肾脏组织逐渐被替代，肾功能持续下降，最终可能发展为肾衰竭。多囊肾由基因突变引起，通常遗传自父母一方。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as CT scan / MRI, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如CT扫描/MRI、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any surgery performed? If yes, please state the date & type of operation.
> 3) 是否进行过手术？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 4) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 5) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 5) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件
