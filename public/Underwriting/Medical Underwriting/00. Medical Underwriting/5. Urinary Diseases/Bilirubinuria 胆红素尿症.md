#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Bilirubinuria is the presence of bilirubin in urine, usually detected during a routine urine dipstick test. Bilirubin is a yellow pigment produced when the liver breaks down red blood cells. Finding it in urine is abnormal and can be an early warning sign of a liver or bile duct problem — sometimes appearing before jaundice becomes visible.
胆红素尿是指尿液中出现胆红素，通常在例行尿液检查中被发现。胆红素是肝脏分解红血球时产生的黄色色素，正常情况下不应出现在尿液中。尿液中检测到胆红素属于异常情况，可能是肝脏或胆道疾病的早期信号，有时在黄疸症状出现之前便可被检测到。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：血液、尿液及扫描检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> During a routine medical check-up on dd/mm/yy, a small amount of bile was detected in my urine test. I was seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. A repeat urine FEME was done 2 weeks later and showed no further bile in urine.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 例行体检时，尿液检查发现少量胆红素，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，2周后复查尿液常规，结果正常，无再次发现胆红素。
> Blood & urine tests were done on dd/mm/yy. Reports are attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。
