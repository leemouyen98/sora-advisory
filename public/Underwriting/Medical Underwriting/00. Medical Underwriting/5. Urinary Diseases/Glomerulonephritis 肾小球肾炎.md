#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Glomerulonephritis is inflammation of the glomeruli — the tiny filtering clusters inside the kidney. It can occur suddenly (acute) or develop gradually over time (chronic). The condition is often triggered by an immune reaction where the body's antibodies attack and damage the kidney's filtering tissue, impairing its ability to clear waste and regulate fluids.
肾小球肾炎是肾脏内微小过滤单位（肾小球）的炎症，可急性发作或慢性进展。此病通常由免疫反应引起，体内抗体攻击并损伤肾脏过滤组织，导致肾脏清除废物和调节体液的功能受损。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood (renal function test) & urine FEME? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、肾功能血液检查及尿液常规检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood（肾功能检查）, urine FEME（尿液常规）& ultrasound scan
> a) 最新相关报告：肾功能血液检查、尿液常规检查及超声波扫描
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Active or frequently recurring — Decline
> - 现有病状或频繁复发 — 拒保
> - Standard / substandard if episode was more than 2 years ago with full recovery and normal kidney ultrasound
> - 若发作已超过2年、完全康复且肾脏超声检查正常，可标准或次标准承保
> - Standard if episode was more than 5 years ago
> - 若发作已超过5年，可标准承保
