#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Any of a group of kidney disease involving the glomeruli, usually thought to be the result of antibody-antigen reactions that localize in the kidneys because of their filtering function.

Glomerulonephritis can come on suddenly (acute) or gradually (chronic).

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such scanning, blood (renal function test) & urine FEME tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 scanning, blood (renal function test) & urine FEME tests etc...）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered ?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生

> a) 任何相关报告，如 blood (renal function test) / urine FEME / Ultrasound scan test 。

> 2) Medical Examination & Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果可能性

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> 人寿保险 / 全残保险 / 严重疾病保险 / 医疗保险：
> – Present & frequent recurrence – Decline case
> – 现有病状且频繁复发 – 拒保
> – Can be standard / substandard if the episode more than 2 years with full recovery, normal ultrasound of kidney
> – 若发作已超过2年、完全康复且肾脏超声检查正常，可标准或次标准承保
> – Can be standard if the episode more than 5 years
> – 若发作已超过5年，可标准承保
