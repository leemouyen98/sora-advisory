#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Nephrotic syndrome is a kidney disorder where the filtering membranes become so damaged that large amounts of protein leak into the urine. This leads to low blood protein levels and widespread tissue swelling (oedema). It is most commonly caused by glomerulonephritis. Prognosis is generally good, but untreated cases can become severe or life-threatening depending on the underlying cause.
肾病综合征是一种肾脏疾病，因肾脏过滤膜受损，大量蛋白质从尿液流失，导致血液中蛋白质水平下降及全身组织水肿。最常见的病因是肾小球肾炎。整体预后通常较好，但若不及时治疗，病情可能因潜在病因而恶化甚至危及生命。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood (renal function test) & urine FEME? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、肾功能血液检查及尿液常规检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood（肾功能检查）, urine FEME（尿液常规）& ultrasound scan
> a) 最新相关报告：肾功能血液检查、尿液常规检查及超声波扫描
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Active or frequently recurring — Decline
> - 现有病状或频繁复发 — 拒保
> - Standard / substandard if episode was more than 2 years ago with full recovery and normal kidney ultrasound
> - 若发作已超过2年、完全康复且肾脏超声检查正常，可标准或次标准承保
> - Standard if episode was more than 5 years ago
> - 若发作已超过5年，可标准承保
