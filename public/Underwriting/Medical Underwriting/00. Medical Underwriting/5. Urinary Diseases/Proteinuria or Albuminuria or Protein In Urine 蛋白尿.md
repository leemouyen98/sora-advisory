#Medical_Underwriting/Urinary_Diseases

Proteinuria is the presence of elevated levels of protein in the urine. Proteins help build muscle and bone, regulate fluid balance in the blood, fight infections, and repair tissue — they should not appear in significant amounts in urine. Their presence is a potential indicator of kidney damage or disease and requires further investigation.
蛋白尿是指尿液中蛋白质含量异常偏高。蛋白质在人体中承担多种重要功能，包括构建肌肉和骨骼、调节血液体液平衡、抵御感染及修复组织，正常情况下不应大量出现在尿液中。尿液中出现蛋白质，可能是肾脏受损或患病的信号，需进一步检查。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as scanning, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如扫描、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：血液、尿液及扫描检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> During a routine medical check-up on dd/mm/yy, a small amount of protein was found in my urine test. I was seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. A repeat urine FEME 2 weeks later showed no further protein in urine.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 例行体检时，尿液检查发现少量蛋白质，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，2周后复查尿液常规，尿液中无再次发现蛋白质。
> Blood & urine tests were done on dd/mm/yy. Reports are attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。
