#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Is a ulcer that develops in the first part of the small intestine (duodenum). Common symptom is pain in the upper tummy (abdomen) just below the breastbone (sternum). It usually comes and goes. Other symptoms which may occur include bloating, retching and feeling sick.

# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such endoscopy (OGDS), blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

# COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor  
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> 
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

# EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have duodenal ulcer on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy hospital. OGDS been performed, report attached.
> On medical follow up & medication
