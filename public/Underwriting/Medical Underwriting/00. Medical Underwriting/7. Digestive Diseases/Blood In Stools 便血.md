#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Means there is bleeding somewhere in your digestive tract. Bright red blood in the stool typically indicates that there is bleeding in the rectum or colon, which may be a sign of colon or rectal cancer. Rectal bleeding can also be caused by haemorrhoids (piles).

# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
# 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

# COMMON REQUIREMENTS
# 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> a) 任何相关报告，如血液/尿液/扫描检查？
>
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

# EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
# 披露示例

> Noticed to have bit of blood in the stool on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy hospital. Suspected digestive tract problem, OGDS been performed, report attached.
> dd/mm/yy 发现大便带血，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，疑消化道问题，已进行胃镜检查，报告随附。
> On medical follow up & medication.
> 进行医疗随访及服药。

# POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
# 核保结果可能性

> Life / TPD/ CI / Medical insurance:
> 人寿保险 / 全残保险 / 严重疾病保险 / 医疗保险：
> – Postpone till fully investigated & firm diagnosis has been established
> – 延迟至全面检查并确立明确诊断
