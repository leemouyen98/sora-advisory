#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

Also known as gastric ulcers, are open sores that develop on the lining of the stomach. Stomach ulcer pain usually begins in the upper middle part of the abdomen, above the belly button and below the breastbone. The pain may feel like burning that may go through to the back. The onset of the pain may occur several hours after a meal when the stomach is empty.

# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such endoscopy (OGDS), blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
# COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor  
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> 
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
