#Medical_Underwriting/Digestive_Diseases

It causes inflammation to the digestive tract, which can lead to abdominal pain, severe diarrhea, fatigue, weight loss and malnutrition.

Crohn’s disease is a long-term inflammatory bowel disease (IBD). It most commonly affects the ileum, which is the end section of the small intestine, and the first section of the large intestine, or colon.

# FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
# 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such endoscopy, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如内镜、血液及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

# COMMON REQUIREMENTS
# 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> a) 任何相关报告，如血液/尿液/扫描检查？
>
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

# EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
# 披露示例

> Diagnosed to crohn’s disease on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy hospital. OGDS & Colonoscopy has been performed. Well discharged. Report is attached.
> dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有克罗恩病。已完成胃镜（OGDS）及结肠镜检查，康复出院，报告随附。
> On regular medical follow up.
> 进行定期医疗随访。
