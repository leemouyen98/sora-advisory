#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Thalassemia is an inherited blood disorder where the body makes less haemoglobin than normal, reducing the red blood cells' ability to carry oxygen. It ranges in severity — mild forms (thalassemia trait/carrier) may cause little to no symptoms, while severe forms require regular blood transfusions.
地中海贫血是一种遗传性血液疾病，患者体内血红蛋白的生产量低于正常水平，降低了红血胞的输氧能力。严重程度因人而异——轻型（携带者）症状轻微甚至无症状，而重型则需要定期输血。

Alpha thalassemia has four types based on the number of affected gene copies: silent carrier (1 gene), carrier (2 genes), Haemoglobin H disease (3 genes), and alpha thalassemia major (all 4 genes affected — most severe).
甲型地中海贫血依受影响基因数量分为四型：无声携带者（1个基因）、携带者（2个基因）、血红蛋白H病（3个基因）及甲型重型地中海贫血（全部4个基因受影响，最为严重）。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. alpha thalassemia trait, beta thalassemia major)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如甲型携带者、乙型重型地中海贫血）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. haemoglobin electrophoresis, full blood count, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如血红蛋白电泳、全血细胞计数、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. blood transfusion, iron chelation)? If yes, what type and how often?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如输血、去铁治疗）？如是，说明类型及频率。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: haemoglobin electrophoresis（血红蛋白电泳）, full blood count（全血细胞计数）, blood tests
> a) 最新报告：血红蛋白电泳、全血细胞计数、血液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with thalassemia trait (alpha, carrier) on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断为甲型地中海贫血携带者。
> Haemoglobin level within normal range. Annual medical follow-up. Investigation reports dated dd/mm/yy attached.
> 血红蛋白水平在正常范围内。每年进行例行随访。dd/mm/yy 的检查报告随附。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if thalassemia trait (carrier) with haemoglobin (Hb) within normal range
> - 若为地中海贫血携带者且血红蛋白（Hb）在正常范围内，标准承保
> - Moderate to severe cases: depends on investigation results and treatment required
> - 中重度病例：视检查结果及所需治疗而定
