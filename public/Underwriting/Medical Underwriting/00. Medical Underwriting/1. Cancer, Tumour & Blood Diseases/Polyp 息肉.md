#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

A polyp is a benign (non-cancerous) growth on the lining of a hollow organ — most commonly found in the colon, but can also occur in the nose, uterus, stomach, or bladder. They vary in size and most cause no symptoms. Large polyps (over 5mm) are typically removed as a precaution because some types can turn cancerous over time.
息肉是空腔器官内壁上的良性增生，最常见于结肠，也可出现在鼻腔、子宫、胃部或膀胱。大小不一，大多无明显症状。较大的息肉（超过5毫米）通常会被切除，以防止部分类型的息肉日后恶变。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type / location of polyp?
> 1) 何时确诊？息肉的类型及位置？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. colonoscopy, biopsy/HPE, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如结肠镜、活检/HPE、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was it removed? If yes, state date and method.
> 3) 是否已切除？如是，请说明日期及切除方式。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 6) Fully recovered?
> 6) 是否已完全康复？

> 人寿 / 全残：
> - Standard if HPE/biopsy report shows negative (benign) result

>
> CI / Medical:
> 严重疾病 / 医疗：
