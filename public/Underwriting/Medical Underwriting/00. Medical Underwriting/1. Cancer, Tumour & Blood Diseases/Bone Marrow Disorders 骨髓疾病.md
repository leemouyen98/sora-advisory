#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Bone marrow is the spongy tissue inside certain bones that produces blood cells — red cells for carrying oxygen, white cells for fighting infection, and platelets for clotting. Bone marrow disorders occur when this production process breaks down, either due to genetics or environmental damage.
骨髓是位于某些骨骼内部的海绵状组织，负责生产红血胞（输氧）、白血胞（抗感染）和血小板（止血）。骨髓疾病是指这一生产过程因遗传或环境因素而出现异常。

Common types include leukaemia (too many abnormal white cells), aplastic anaemia (too few red cells), and myeloproliferative disorders (overproduction of white cells).
常见类型包括白血病（异常白血胞过多）、再生障碍性贫血（红血胞过少）及骨髓增殖性疾病（白血胞过多）。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and by which doctor / hospital?
> 1) 何时确诊？由哪位医生 / 哪家医院诊断？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. bone marrow biopsy, full blood count, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如骨髓活检、全血细胞计数、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. chemotherapy, stem cell transplant)? If yes, what type and duration?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如化疗、干细胞移植）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: bone marrow biopsy（骨髓活检）, full blood count（全血细胞计数）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：骨髓活检、全血细胞计数、血液/尿液检查
> b) Operation / transfusion report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/输血报告
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
