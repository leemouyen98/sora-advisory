#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Lymph nodes are small glands found throughout the body that play a key role in the immune system. They commonly swell in response to infection (bacterial or viral) as the body fights off illness. In rare cases, enlarged lymph nodes may signal a more serious condition such as lymphoma or other cancers.
淋巴结是分布于全身的小型腺体，在免疫系统中扮演重要角色。淋巴结肿大最常见的原因是细菌或病毒感染，是身体对抗疾病的正常反应。少数情况下，持续肿大的淋巴结可能提示淋巴瘤等较严重疾病。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it first noticed, and where on the body?
> 1) 何时首次发现？位于身体哪个部位？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. biopsy/HPE, CT scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如活检/HPE、CT扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was it removed surgically? If yes, state date and type of procedure.
> 3) 是否已手术切除？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 6) Fully recovered?
> 6) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, CT scan（CT扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：活检/病理报告、CT扫描、血液/尿液检查
> b) Operation / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Enlarged lymph node noticed on dd/mm/yy and reviewed by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 发现淋巴结肿大，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。
> Confirmed as reactive — caused by fever. One follow-up on dd/mm/yy.
> 确认为反应性肿大，原因为发烧。进行了一次 dd/mm/yy 的随访。
> Blood and urine tests dated dd/mm/yy attached. Fully recovered since.
> dd/mm/yy 的血液及尿液检查报告随附。自此已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD:
> 人寿 / 全残：
> - Standard if investigation results (including HPE) are negative
> - 检查结果（包括病理报告）呈阴性者，标准承保
>
> CI / Medical:
> 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Depends on investigation results
> - 视检查结果而定
> - Postpone if diagnosis has not been confirmed
> - 若尚未确立明确诊断，延迟承保
