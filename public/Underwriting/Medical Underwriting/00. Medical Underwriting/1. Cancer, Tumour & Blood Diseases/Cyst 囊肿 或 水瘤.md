#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

A cyst is an abnormal, usually harmless sac filled with fluid or semi-solid material. Cysts can form anywhere in the body and most are benign. They may need to be surgically removed if they grow large, cause pain, or interfere with normal tissue function.
囊肿是一种充满液体或半固体物质的异常囊状物，通常无害，可在身体任何部位形成，大多数为良性。若囊肿增大、引起疼痛或影响组织功能，则可能需要手术切除。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type of cyst (e.g. ovarian, sebaceous, ganglion)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种囊肿（如卵巢囊肿、皮脂腺囊肿、腱鞘囊肿）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. ultrasound, biopsy/HPE, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如超声波、活检/HPE、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was it removed surgically? If yes, state date and type of procedure.
> 3) 是否已手术切除？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 6) Fully recovered?
> 6) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: ultrasound scan（超声波扫描）, biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声波扫描、活检/病理报告、血液/尿液检查
> b) Operation / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
> c) Growth Questionnaire completed by applicant
> c) 申请人填写生长问卷
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with ovarian cyst on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有卵巢囊肿。
> Completely removed by surgery on dd/mm/yy. HPE, blood and urine tests dated dd/mm/yy attached.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 手术完全切除。dd/mm/yy 的病理检查（HPE）、血液及尿液检查报告随附。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD:
> 人寿 / 全残：
> - Standard if HPE/biopsy report shows negative (benign) result
> - 活检/病理报告呈阴性（良性）者，标准承保
>
> CI / Medical:
> 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Exclusion if HPE/biopsy report shows negative (benign) result
> - 活检/病理报告呈阴性（良性）者，除外承保
