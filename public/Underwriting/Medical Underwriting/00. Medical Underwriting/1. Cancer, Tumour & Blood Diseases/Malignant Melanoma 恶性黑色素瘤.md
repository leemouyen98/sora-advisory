#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Malignant melanoma is the most dangerous form of skin cancer, developing from the pigment-producing cells (melanocytes) in the skin. It can also occur less commonly in the eye (uveal melanoma), mouth, or intestines. Unlike other skin cancers, melanoma spreads aggressively if not detected early.
恶性黑色素瘤是最危险的皮肤癌，起源于皮肤中产生色素的黑色素细胞。较少情况下也可发生在眼睛（葡萄膜黑色素瘤）、口腔或肠道。与其他皮肤癌不同，黑色素瘤若未及早发现，扩散速度极快。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and at what stage?
> 1) 何时确诊？处于哪个阶段？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. biopsy/HPE, CT scan, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如活检/HPE、CT扫描、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. surgery, immunotherapy, targeted therapy)? If yes, what type and duration?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如手术、免疫治疗、靶向治疗）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, CT scan（CT扫描）, blood tests
> a) 最新报告：活检/病理报告、CT扫描、血液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
