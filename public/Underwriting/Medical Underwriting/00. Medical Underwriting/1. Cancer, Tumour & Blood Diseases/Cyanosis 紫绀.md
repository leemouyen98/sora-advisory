#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Cyanosis is a bluish-purple discolouration of the skin, most visible on the lips, fingernails, and earlobes. It indicates reduced oxygen levels in the blood and usually signals an underlying heart or lung problem. It becomes clinically visible when blood oxygen saturation drops to around 80–85%.
紫绀是指皮肤出现蓝紫色变色，尤其在嘴唇、指甲和耳垂处最为明显。这表示血液中氧气含量不足，通常提示心脏或肺部存在潜在问题。当血氧饱和度下降至约80–85%时，紫绀开始明显可见。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what was the underlying cause?
> 1) 何时确诊？潜在原因是什么？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. blood oxygen test, chest X-ray, ECG, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如血氧检测、胸部X光、心电图、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: blood oxygen saturation（血氧饱和度）, chest X-ray（胸部X光）, ECG（心电图）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：血氧饱和度、胸部X光、心电图、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with cyanosis on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Cause identified as respiratory infection.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有紫绀，原因为呼吸道感染。
> Investigation reports dated dd/mm/yy attached. Fully recovered after treatment.
> dd/mm/yy 的检查报告随附。治疗后已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Depends on investigation results and underlying cause
> - 视检查结果及病因而定
> - Postpone / decline if cause is unknown or case is severe
> - 若病因不明或病情严重，延迟或拒绝承保
