#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

A pre-malignant condition (also called a precancerous condition) involves abnormal cells that have not yet become cancerous but carry a significantly higher risk of doing so. Examples include cervical dysplasia, Barrett's oesophagus, actinic keratosis, and certain polyps. Early detection and treatment can prevent progression to cancer.
癌前病变（又称癌前状态）是指细胞出现异常但尚未发展为癌症的状态，但其演变为癌症的风险明显较高。常见例子包括宫颈不典型增生、巴雷特食管、光化性角化病及某些类型的息肉。早期发现和治疗可防止其进展为癌症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what specific pre-malignant condition?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种癌前病变？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. biopsy/HPE, Pap smear, endoscopy, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如活检/HPE、子宫颈涂片、内窥镜、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment? If yes, what type and duration?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, Pap smear（子宫颈涂片）, endoscopy（内窥镜）, blood tests
> a) 最新报告：活检/病理报告、子宫颈涂片、内窥镜、血液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
