#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Haemophilia is an inherited bleeding disorder where the blood lacks sufficient clotting factors, causing wounds to bleed for much longer than normal. Sufferers may also experience spontaneous internal bleeding into joints and muscles. There is currently no cure, but treatment with clotting factor replacement therapy allows most patients to live relatively normal lives.
血友病是一种遗传性出血疾病，患者血液中缺乏足够的凝血因子，导致伤口出血时间异常延长，关节和肌肉也可能自发性出血。目前尚无根治方法，但注射凝血因子替代治疗可让大多数患者过上相对正常的生活。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. Haemophilia A, B)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如甲型、乙型血友病）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. clotting factor levels, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如凝血因子水平、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. clotting factor replacement)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如凝血因子替代治疗）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: clotting factor assay（凝血因子检测）, full blood count（全血细胞计数）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：凝血因子检测、全血细胞计数、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
