A lump is a localised area of swelling or protuberance that can appear anywhere on the body. Lumps may result from infections, inflammation, benign growths, or tumours. Most lumps are benign, but any unexplained lump should be investigated to rule out malignancy.
肿块是身体任何部位出现的局部隆起或肿胀，可能由感染、炎症、良性增生或肿瘤引起。大多数肿块属良性，但任何原因不明的肿块均应进行检查，以排除恶性的可能。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it first noticed, and where on the body?
> 1) 何时首次发现？位于身体哪个部位？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. ultrasound, biopsy/HPE, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如超声波、活检/HPE、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was it removed surgically? If yes, state date and type of procedure.
> 3) 是否已手术切除？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 6) Fully recovered?
> 6) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: ultrasound scan（超声波扫描）, biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声波扫描、活检/病理报告、血液/尿液检查
> b) Operation / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
> c) Growth Questionnaire completed by applicant
> c) 申请人填写生长问卷
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD:
> 人寿 / 全残：
> - Standard if HPE/biopsy report shows negative (benign) result
> - 活检/病理报告呈阴性（良性）者，标准承保
>
> CI / Medical:
> 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Exclusion if HPE/biopsy report shows negative (benign) result
> - 活检/病理报告呈阴性（良性）者，除外承保
