#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

A pheochromocytoma is a rare tumour that forms in the adrenal glands — the two small glands that sit on top of each kidney. Most pheochromocytomas are benign (non-cancerous). The tumour causes the adrenal glands to produce excess adrenaline and noradrenaline, leading to symptoms like high blood pressure, severe headaches, sweating, and rapid heartbeat.
嗜铬细胞瘤是一种罕见的肾上腺肿瘤——肾上腺位于两侧肾脏顶端。大多数嗜铬细胞瘤为良性。肿瘤会导致肾上腺过度分泌肾上腺素和去甲肾上腺素，引发高血压、剧烈头痛、出汗及心跳加速等症状。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and is it benign or malignant?
> 1) 何时确诊？是良性还是恶性？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. biopsy/HPE, CT/MRI scan, urine catecholamine test, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如活检/HPE、CT/MRI扫描、尿液儿茶酚胺检测、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was it removed surgically? If yes, state date and type of procedure.
> 3) 是否已手术切除？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, urine catecholamine test（尿液儿茶酚胺检测）, blood tests
> a) 最新报告：活检/病理报告、CT/MRI扫描、尿液儿茶酚胺检测、血液检查
> b) Operation / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## 披露示例
