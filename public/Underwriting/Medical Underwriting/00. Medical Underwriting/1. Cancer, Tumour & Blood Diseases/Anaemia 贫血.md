#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Anaemia means your body doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells to carry oxygen to your tissues. This can leave you feeling tired, weak, and short of breath. There are many types — the most common is iron deficiency anaemia, where a lack of iron prevents the body from making enough haemoglobin.
贫血是指身体缺乏足够健康的红血胞来输送氧气，导致疲倦、虚弱和气促。类型众多，最常见的是缺铁性贫血——缺铁使身体无法制造足够的血红蛋白。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and by which doctor / hospital?
> 1) 何时确诊？由哪位医生 / 哪家医院诊断？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. full blood count, iron studies, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如全血细胞计数、铁质检查、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: full blood count (全血细胞计数), iron studies (铁质检查), blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：全血细胞计数、铁质检查、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with iron deficiency anaemia on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有缺铁性贫血。
> On daily iron supplement. Investigation reports dated dd/mm/yy attached.
> 每日服用铁剂补充。dd/mm/yy 的检查报告随附。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if haemoglobin (Hb) is within normal range
> - 血红蛋白（Hb）在正常范围内者，标准承保
> - Depends on investigation results and underlying cause
> - 视检查结果及病因而定
