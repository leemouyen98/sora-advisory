#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

A rash is an area of irritated, red, swollen, or discoloured skin. Rashes can be itchy, painful, or produce blisters. They are a symptom of many conditions — ranging from minor allergic reactions and infections to more serious systemic diseases. The underlying cause determines treatment and underwriting approach.
皮疹是皮肤出现刺激、泛红、肿胀或变色的状况，可伴有瘙痒、疼痛或水疱。皮疹是多种疾病的症状，从轻微的过敏反应和感染到较严重的全身性疾病均可引起。潜在原因决定治疗方向及核保处理方式。

Skin diseases are caused by a wide variety of germs and can range from mild (treatable at home) to serious (requiring medical treatment). Common examples include urticaria (hives), eczema, psoriasis, and fungal infections.
皮肤病由多种病原体引起，轻者可自行处理，重者需要就医。常见例子包括荨麻疹、湿疹、牛皮癣及真菌感染。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type of rash or skin condition?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种皮疹或皮肤病？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. skin biopsy, allergy test, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如皮肤活检、过敏测试、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: skin biopsy（皮肤活检）, allergy test（过敏测试）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：皮肤活检、过敏测试、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with urticaria (hives) on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Clinic.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 诊所 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有荨麻疹。
> Medication given for 3 days. One follow-up on dd/mm/yy. Fully recovered since.
> 给予3天药物治疗。进行了一次 dd/mm/yy 的随访。自此已完全康复。

## 核保结果

> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if investigation results are negative and fully recovered
> - 检查结果呈阴性且完全康复者，标准承保
