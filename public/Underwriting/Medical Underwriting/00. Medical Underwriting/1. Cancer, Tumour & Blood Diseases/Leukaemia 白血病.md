#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Leukaemia is a group of blood cancers that originate in the bone marrow, where abnormal white blood cells are produced in excess and crowd out healthy blood cells. Common symptoms include unusual or frequent bleeding (such as bleeding gums or nosebleeds), easy bruising, fatigue, and flat red or purple spots on the skin.
白血病是一组起源于骨髓的血液癌症，骨髓产生过多异常白血胞并排挤正常血细胞。常见症状包括异常或频繁出血（如牙龈出血、流鼻血）、容易瘀伤、疲倦，以及皮肤出现红色或紫色斑点。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. AML, ALL, CML, CLL)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如急性髓性、急性淋巴、慢性髓性、慢性淋巴白血病）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. bone marrow biopsy, full blood count, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如骨髓活检、全血细胞计数、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. chemotherapy, stem cell transplant)? If yes, what type and duration?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如化疗、干细胞移植）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: bone marrow biopsy（骨髓活检）, full blood count（全血细胞计数）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：骨髓活检、全血细胞计数、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
