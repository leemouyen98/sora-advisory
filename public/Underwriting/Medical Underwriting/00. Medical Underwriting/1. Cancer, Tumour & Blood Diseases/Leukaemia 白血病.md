#Medical_Underwriting/Cancer_Tumour_Blood_Diseases

Is the general name given to a group of cancers that develop in the bone marrow. Leukaemia originates in developing blood cells that have undergone a malignant change. Common symptoms are unusual and frequent bleeding, such as bleeding gums or nosebleeds, easily bruised skin, flat red or purple spots on the skin.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such bone morrow biopsy (HPE), blood & urine tests etc done ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 bone morrow biopsy (HPE), blood & urine tests etc done）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
