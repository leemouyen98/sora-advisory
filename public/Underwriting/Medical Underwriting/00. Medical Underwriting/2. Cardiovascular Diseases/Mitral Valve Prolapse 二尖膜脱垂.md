#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Mitral valve prolapse (MVP) occurs when the leaflets of the mitral valve billow backward into the left atrium during heart contraction. Most cases are mild and require no treatment. However, some cases lead to mitral regurgitation, arrhythmias and, rarely, serious cardiac complications.
二尖瓣脱垂（MVP）是指二尖瓣瓣叶在心脏收缩时向左心房方向脱出。大多数病例症状轻微，无需治疗。但部分病例可导致二尖瓣反流、心律失常，极少数情况下可引发严重心脏并发症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and severity (mild / moderate / severe)?
> 1) 确诊日期及严重程度（轻度 / 中度 / 重度）？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., echocardiogram, ECG, chest X-ray, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如超声心动图、心电图、胸部X光、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is there associated mitral regurgitation (valve leaking)? If yes, what is the severity?
> 3) 是否伴有二尖瓣反流（瓣膜漏血）？如有，严重程度如何？
> 4) Has any surgery been performed? If yes, please state the type, date and outcome.
> 4) 是否曾接受手术？如有，请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 5) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 5) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 6) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 6) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: echocardiogram, ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声心动图、心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
> b) Surgical / procedure report (if applicable)
> b) 手术或治疗程序报告（如有）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Decline in most cases, particularly if associated with significant regurgitation or arrhythmia.
> - 大多数情况下拒保，尤其是伴有明显反流或心律失常的病例。
> - Mild isolated MVP with no regurgitation may be considered individually.
> - 无反流的单纯轻度二尖瓣脱垂可个案评估。
