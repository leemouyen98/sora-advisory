#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Hypertension (high blood pressure) is a chronic condition where blood pressure remains persistently elevated, typically at or above 140/90 mmHg. Over time it damages the heart, kidneys, eyes and brain. It is one of the most common disclosures in insurance applications. With good BP control and no organ damage, standard rates are achievable.
高血压是指血压持续偏高（通常在140/90 mmHg 或以上）的慢性病症，长期高血压会损伤心脏、肾脏、眼睛及脑部。这是保险申请中最常见的申报事项之一。若血压控制良好且无器官损伤，可以标准费率承保。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS – HYPERTENSION
## 常见问题 – 高血压

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis?
> 1) 确诊日期？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Are there any contributing factors (e.g., overweight, diabetes, smoking)?
> 3) 是否有诱发因素（如超重、糖尿病、吸烟）？
> 4) Any hospital admission history? If yes, when and for how long?
> 4) 是否有住院记录？如有，请说明时间及住院时长。
> 5) What medication is the client currently on?
> 5) 客户目前服用哪种药物？
> 6) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 6) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS – HYPERTENSION
## 所需文件 – 高血压

> From client:
> 来自客户：
> a) Latest relevant reports: ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新相关报告：心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
> b) Hypertension questionnaire completed by applicant
> b) 申请人填写的高血压问卷
>
> Medical examination by doctor with 3 blood pressure readings.
> 由医生进行体格检查，并测量三次血压。
>
> Hypertension questionnaire by treating doctor (required depending on admission history and sum assured).
> 主治医生填写高血压问卷（视住院记录及保额而定）。

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE – HYPERTENSION
## 披露示例 – 高血压

> Diagnosed with hypertension on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Clinic. Currently on regular follow-up and medication. Last blood pressure reading: 130/85 on dd/mm/yy. No hospital admission. Blood and urine test done on dd/mm/yy — reports attached.
> 由 yy 诊所 Dr xxx 医生于 dd/mm/yy 诊断患有高血压，目前接受定期随访及药物治疗。最近一次血压读数为 dd/mm/yy 的 130/85，迄今无需住院。dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES – HYPERTENSION
## 核保结果 – 高血压

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if BP is well controlled with no medical complications.
> - 血压控制良好且无医学并发症者，可标准承保。

# High Blood Pressure 血压高

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE
## 常见问题 – 血压偏高

> 1) When did it first occur and how many episodes have there been?
> 1) 首次发病日期及发作次数？
> 2) Were there any symptoms (e.g., dizziness, headache)?
> 2) 是否出现任何症状（如头晕、头痛）？
> 3) Have blood or urine tests been done?
> 3) 是否进行过血液及尿液检查？
> 4) Are there any contributing factors (e.g., stress, insomnia)?
> 4) 是否有诱发因素（如压力、失眠）？
> 5) Is the client on medication or lifestyle advice only?
> 5) 客户是在服药还是仅接受生活方式建议？
> 6) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 6) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE
## 所需文件 – 血压偏高

> From client:
> 来自客户：
> a) Latest relevant reports: ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新相关报告：心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
>
> Medical examination by doctor with 3 blood pressure readings.
> 由医生进行体格检查，并测量三次血压。

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE
## 披露示例 – 血压偏高

> 1 episode of raised blood pressure (145/95) on dd/mm/yy due to work stress. Advised to relax and exercise more, no medication required. Repeat reading on dd/mm/yy was 130/90 — fully resolved with lifestyle changes.
> dd/mm/yy 因工作压力出现1次血压升高（145/95），建议放松及多运动，无需药物治疗。dd/mm/yy 复查读数为130/90，以生活方式调整后已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE
## 核保结果 – 血压偏高

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if underlying cause identified, 3 BP readings are within normal range, and no medical complications.
> - 若已确定根本原因、三次血压读数均在正常范围内且无医学并发症，可标准承保。
