## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS – HYPERTENSION

> 1) Date of diagnosis ?  
> 2) Any chest x-ray (CXR), ECG, blood & urine test done ?  
> 3) Any triggering disease (overweight / diabetes /smoking etc) ? 
> 4) Any history of admission (within 2 years/above 2 years)?  
> 5) On what type of medication ?  
> 6) Still on medical follow up, if yes, how frequent ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS – HYPERTENSION

> 1) From client  
> a) Any relevant reports such as ECG, X-ray, blood & urine test. b) Hypertension questionnaire by applicant
> 
> 1) Medical examination by doctor with 3 times blood pressure measurement.
> 
> 3) Hypertension questionnaire by attending doctor. (KIV – depends on history of admission/sum assured)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE – HYPERTENSION

> Diagnosed to have hypertension on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy klinik, currently on regular doctor follow up & medication. Last BP checked was 130/85 on dd/mm/yy.
> No admission required so far.  
> Blood & urine test done on dd/mm/yy is attached.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES – HYPERTENSION

> Life / TPD /CI / Medical insurance:
> - Can be standard case provided good BP control & no medical complication.

# High Blood Pressure 血压高
## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE

> 1. Date of onset (how many episode)?
> 2. Any symptoms arises ? Exp : dizziness/headache
> 3. Any blood & urine test done ?
> 4. Any triggering disease (stress / insomnia, etc...) ?
> 5. On any type of medication or advises only ?
> 6. Still on medical follow up, if yes, how frequent ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE

> 1) From client  
> a) any relevant reports such as ECG, X-ray, blood & urine test
> 
> 2) Medical examination by doctor with 3 times blood pressure measurement.

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE

> History of 1 episode of raised blood pressure(145/95) due to stress at work on dd/mm/yy. Advised to relax & more exercises, no medication needed.
> Repeated 130/90 on dd/mm/yy, fully recovered with normal lifestyle.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES – HIGH BLOOD PRESSURE

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> - Possibility of standard case provided underlying causes have been defined, 3 Blood Pressure measurement are within normal limit & no medical complications.
