#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Is a type of heart valve disease that involves the narrowing of the pulmonary valve, which controls the flow of blood from the heart’s right ventricle into the pulmonary artery to carry blood to the lungs.
To compensate, the heart has to work harder to pump enough blood through the valve, and to the body. Pulmonary stenosis can be mild, moderate, severe or life-threatening.
Treatment options for pulmonary stenosis include open-heart surgery or balloon angioplasty. The primary indication for treatment is the degree of narrowing and treatment is timed to prevent damage to the right heart.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果可能性

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> 人寿保险 / 全残保险 / 严重疾病保险 / 医疗保险：
> - Depends on the severity (mild / moderate / severe) and outcomes of investigation reports.
> - 取决于严重程度（轻度 / 中度 / 重度）及检查报告结果。
> - Mild cases with no symptoms and normal heart function: Standard or rated.
> - 轻度无症状且心脏功能正常：标准或额外费率承保。
> - Moderate to severe cases or post-surgical: Rated, exclusion, or postponed depending on current cardiac status.
> - 中度至重度或术后病例：根据目前心脏状况，可能以额外费率、除外承保或延迟承保处理。
