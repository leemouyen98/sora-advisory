#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Pulmonary stenosis is a narrowing of the pulmonary valve, which controls blood flow from the right ventricle to the lungs. This forces the right ventricle to work harder to push blood through. Severity ranges from mild (often no symptoms) to severe (causing breathlessness, fatigue and right heart failure). Treatment includes balloon valvuloplasty or surgery depending on severity.
肺动脉瓣狭窄是指控制右心室血液流向肺部的肺动脉瓣变窄，迫使右心室更用力泵血。严重程度从轻度（通常无症状）到重度（引起气促、疲劳及右心衰竭）不等。治疗方式包括球囊瓣膜成形术或手术，视严重程度而定。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and severity (mild / moderate / severe)?
> 1) 确诊日期及严重程度（轻度 / 中度 / 重度）？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., ECG, chest X-ray, echocardiogram, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如心电图、胸部X光、超声心动图、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Has any surgery or balloon procedure been performed? If yes, please state the type, date and outcome.
> 3) 是否曾接受手术或球囊扩张术？如有，请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 4) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 5) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 5) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Outcome depends on severity and investigation results.
> - 核保结果取决于严重程度及检查结果。
> - Mild cases with no symptoms and normal right heart function: standard or rated.
> - 轻度无症状且右心功能正常：标准承保或额外费率。
> - Moderate to severe cases, or post-surgical cases: rated, exclusion or postponed depending on current cardiac status.
> - 中度至重度，或术后病例：根据目前心脏状况，可能以额外费率、除外承保或延迟承保处理。
