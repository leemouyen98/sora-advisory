#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Cardiomyopathy is a disease of the heart muscle that makes it harder for the heart to pump blood efficiently. It can lead to heart failure, arrhythmias and sudden cardiac death. Common types include dilated (enlarged heart), hypertrophic (thickened walls) and restrictive cardiomyopathy. This is a serious condition from an underwriting perspective.
心肌病是心肌的疾病，导致心脏难以有效泵血，可引发心脏衰竭、心律失常甚至心脏骤停。主要类型包括扩张型（心脏扩大）、肥厚型（心壁增厚）及限制型心肌病。从核保角度来看，这是一种较为严重的疾病。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and type of cardiomyopathy (dilated / hypertrophic / restrictive)?
> 1) 确诊日期及心肌病类型（扩张型 / 肥厚型 / 限制型）？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., echocardiogram, ECG, cardiac MRI, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如超声心动图、心电图、心脏磁共振、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 4) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 4) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: echocardiogram, ECG, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声心动图、心电图、血液及尿液检查
> b) Surgical / procedure report (if applicable)
> b) 手术或治疗程序报告（如有）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of cardiomyopathy on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Currently on medication. Echocardiogram, chest X-ray and blood tests done on dd/mm/yy — reports attached. Yearly medical follow-up.
> dd/mm/yy 有心肌病病史，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，目前在服药。dd/mm/yy 完成超声心动图、胸部X光及血液检查，报告随附。每年定期复诊。
