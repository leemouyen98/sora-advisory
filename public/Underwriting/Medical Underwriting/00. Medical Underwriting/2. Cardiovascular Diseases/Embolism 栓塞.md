#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Embolism occurs when a blood clot or other material travels through the bloodstream and becomes lodged in a blood vessel, blocking blood flow. The most common types include pulmonary embolism (lungs), cerebral embolism (brain) and peripheral embolism (limb arteries). Embolism is a serious, potentially life-threatening event with a significant risk of recurrence.
栓塞是指血凝块或其他异物随血流运行，堵塞血管导致血流中断。最常见类型包括肺栓塞（肺部）、脑栓塞（脑部）及周围动脉栓塞（肢体动脉）。栓塞属于严重甚至危及生命的事件，且有显著的复发风险。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of the event, type of embolism and location?
> 1) 发病日期、栓塞类型及发生部位？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., CT scan, ECG, echocardiogram, blood tests including D-dimer)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如 CT 扫描、心电图、超声心动图、包括 D 二聚体的血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Has any surgery or thrombolytic treatment been performed? If yes, please state the type, date and outcome.
> 3) 是否曾接受手术或溶栓治疗？如有，请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 4) Is the client currently on anticoagulant medication (e.g., warfarin, apixaban)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 客户目前是否在服用抗凝血药物（如华法林、阿哌沙班）？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 5) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 5) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT scan, ECG, echocardiogram, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT 扫描、心电图、超声心动图、血液及尿液检查
> b) Surgical / procedure report (if applicable)
> b) 手术或治疗程序报告（如有）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of blood vessel embolism on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Procedure performed on dd/mm/yy. Currently on anticoagulant medication. Blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy — reports attached. Yearly follow-up.
> dd/mm/yy 有血管栓塞病史，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，治疗程序于 dd/mm/yy 完成。目前在服用抗凝血药物。dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。每年定期复诊。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Decline in most cases due to high recurrence risk and ongoing anticoagulation requirement.
> - 由于复发风险高且需长期抗凝治疗，大多数情况下拒保。
