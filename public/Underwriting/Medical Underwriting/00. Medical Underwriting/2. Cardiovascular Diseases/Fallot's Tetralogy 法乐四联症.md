#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Fallot's tetralogy is a complex congenital heart condition involving four simultaneous defects: a hole between the lower chambers (VSD), a narrowed pulmonary valve, a thickened right ventricle and an aorta that overrides the VSD. It reduces the oxygen level in the blood and is usually identified at or shortly after birth. Surgical repair is typically performed in infancy and significantly improves long-term outcomes.
法乐四联症是一种复杂的先天性心脏病，同时存在四种缺陷：室间隔缺损（心室间有孔洞）、肺动脉瓣狭窄、右心室肥厚及主动脉骑跨。这会降低血液中的含氧量，通常在出生时或出生后不久被发现。手术修复一般在婴儿期进行，可显著改善长期预后。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and which specific defects are present?
> 1) 确诊日期及存在哪些具体缺陷？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., ECG, chest X-ray, echocardiogram, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如心电图、胸部X光、超声心动图、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Has surgery been performed? If yes, please state the type, date and outcome.
> 3) 是否曾接受手术？如有，请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 4) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 5) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 5) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: echocardiogram, ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声心动图、心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
> b) Surgical report (if surgery was performed)
> b) 手术报告（如已进行手术）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with Fallot's tetralogy at birth. Corrective surgery performed at age 8. Yearly cardiac follow-up ongoing. Latest echocardiogram, chest X-ray and blood tests done on dd/mm/yy — reports attached.
> 出生时确诊法乐四联症，8岁时完成矫正手术。目前每年进行心脏复诊。dd/mm/yy 完成最新超声心动图、胸部X光及血液检查，报告随附。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Outcome depends on post-surgical cardiac status and investigation results.
> - 核保结果取决于术后心脏状况及检查结果。
> - May be standard if surgery was successful with no residual heart defects or complications.
> - 若手术成功且无残留心脏缺陷或并发症，可能标准承保。
