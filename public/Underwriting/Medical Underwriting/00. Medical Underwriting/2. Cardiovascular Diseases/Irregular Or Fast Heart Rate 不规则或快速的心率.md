#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

An irregular or fast heart rate means the heart is beating outside its normal rhythm — either too fast (tachycardia, above 100 bpm at rest) or in an uneven pattern. This is closely related to arrhythmia. The key information needed is the specific confirmed cause, which determines the underwriting outcome.
不规则或快速心率是指心跳节律或频率异常——过快（心动过速，静息时超过100次/分钟）或节律不均。这与心律失常密切相关。核保关键在于确认具体病因，以此决定承保结果。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and the specific confirmed cause?
> 1) 确诊日期及确认的具体病因？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., ECG, Holter monitor, echocardiogram, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如心电图、动态心电监测、超声心动图、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 4) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 4) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: ECG, Holter monitor (if performed), echocardiogram, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：心电图、动态心电监测（如有）、超声心动图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of fast/irregular heart rate on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. ECG, echocardiogram, chest X-ray and blood tests done. Currently on medication. Reports attached. Yearly follow-up.
> dd/mm/yy 有快速/不规则心率病史，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。已完成心电图、超声心动图、胸部X光及血液检查。目前在服药，报告随附，每年定期复诊。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Outcome depends on investigation results and the confirmed underlying cause.
> - 核保结果取决于检查结果及确认的根本原因。
