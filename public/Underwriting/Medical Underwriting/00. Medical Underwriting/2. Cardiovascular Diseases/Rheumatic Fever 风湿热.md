#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Rheumatic fever is an inflammatory disease triggered by an untreated or inadequately treated Group A streptococcal (strep throat) infection. It can cause lasting damage to the heart valves — a condition known as rheumatic heart disease. Multiple episodes significantly increase the risk of permanent valve damage, which can be life-threatening without treatment.
风湿热是由未经治疗或治疗不充分的A组链球菌（化脓性链球菌咽炎）感染引发的炎症性疾病，可造成心脏瓣膜永久性损伤，称为风湿性心脏病。多次发作会显著增加永久性瓣膜损伤的风险，若不治疗可危及生命。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and how many episodes have there been?
> 1) 确诊日期及发作次数？
> 2) Is there any confirmed heart valve damage? What investigations were done (e.g., echocardiogram, ECG, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 是否有确认的心脏瓣膜损伤？曾进行哪些检查（如超声心动图、心电图、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is the client currently on medication (e.g., penicillin prophylaxis)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 客户目前是否在服药（如青霉素预防性治疗）？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 4) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 4) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: echocardiogram, ECG, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声心动图、心电图、血液及尿液检查
> b) Procedure / surgical report (if applicable)
> b) 手术或治疗程序报告（如有）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of rheumatic fever on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Currently on penicillin prophylaxis. Echocardiogram confirmed no valve damage. Blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy — reports attached. Yearly follow-up.
> dd/mm/yy 有风湿热病史，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。目前在服用青霉素预防性治疗，超声心动图确认无瓣膜损伤。dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。每年定期复诊。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Not insurable if any degree of valve damage is confirmed.
> - 若确认有任何程度的瓣膜损伤，不可承保。
> - Standard or substandard if full recovery with no valve damage confirmed by echocardiogram.
> - 若完全康复且超声心动图确认无瓣膜损伤，标准或次标准承保。
