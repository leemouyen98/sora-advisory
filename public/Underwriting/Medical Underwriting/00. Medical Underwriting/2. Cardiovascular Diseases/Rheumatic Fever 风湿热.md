#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Rheumatic fever is an inflammatory disease that can affect many connective tissues, especially in the heart. This infection causes damage the heart valves in a way that keeps the blood from moving through the heart normally.

![[Rheumatic Fever 01.png]]

![Rheumatic Fever with Heart Valve Disorder](https://youtu.be/ZB97F-z36Mk)

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis?
> 2) Any investigation tests such echocardiogram, blood & urine tests etc... done ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any latest relevant reports / results such as blood / urine / scan test, ECG, Echocardiagram... ?
> b) Operation / procedures report if performed.
> 
> 2) Medical examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> History of rheumatic fever dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> Currently on medication.
> Echocardiogram, blood & urine tests were done on dd/mm/yy. Reports are attached here.
> Medical follow up on yearly basis

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES

> Life / TPD / Cl / Medical insurance
> - Not insurable if have some degree of valve damage
