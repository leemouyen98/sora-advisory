#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Varicose veins are swollen, twisted superficial veins visible just under the skin, most commonly in the legs. They occur when the valves inside the veins stop working properly, causing blood to pool and the vein walls to bulge. Most cases are a cosmetic concern or cause mild discomfort. Some can progress to chronic venous insufficiency or skin ulcers if untreated.
静脉曲张是指浅表静脉扩张、扭曲，常见于腿部皮下。当静脉内瓣膜功能减退、血液积聚时，静脉壁随之膨出。大多数病例属于美观问题或轻微不适，若不治疗，部分可发展为慢性静脉功能不全或皮肤溃疡。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and which leg(s) are affected?
> 1) 确诊日期及哪侧腿部受影响？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., Doppler ultrasound, blood & urine tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如多普勒超声波、血液及尿液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Has any surgery been performed (e.g., ligation, stripping, laser treatment)? If yes, please state the type, date and outcome.
> 3) 是否曾接受手术（如结扎、剥脱术、激光治疗）？如有，请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 4) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 5) Is the client fully recovered or still symptomatic?
> 5) 客户是否已完全康复，还是仍有症状？
> 6) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 6) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？
