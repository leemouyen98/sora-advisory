#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Portal hypertension is elevated pressure within the portal vein system, which carries blood from the digestive organs to the liver. It is most commonly caused by liver cirrhosis (scarring of the liver). The increased pressure forces blood to bypass the liver through collateral vessels, leading to an enlarged spleen, varices (swollen veins) in the oesophagus and stomach, and fluid build-up in the abdomen (ascites).
门静脉高血压是指将消化器官血液输送至肝脏的门静脉系统压力升高，最常见的原因是肝硬化（肝脏纤维化）。压力升高迫使血液通过侧支血管绕过肝脏，导致脾脏肿大、食管及胃部静脉曲张（血管扩张），以及腹腔积液（腹水）。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and confirmed underlying cause (e.g., liver cirrhosis, hepatitis)?
> 1) 确诊日期及确认的根本病因（如肝硬化、肝炎）？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., ultrasound, endoscopy, blood tests including liver function)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如超声波、内窥镜、包括肝功能的血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 4) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 4) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: ultrasound / CT scan, endoscopy report, blood tests (including liver function and full blood count)
> a) 最新报告：超声波 / CT 扫描、内窥镜报告、血液检查（包括肝功能及全血计数）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）
