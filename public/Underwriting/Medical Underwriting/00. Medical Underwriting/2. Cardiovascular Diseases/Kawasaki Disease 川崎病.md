#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

Kawasaki disease is an acute inflammatory illness primarily affecting children under 5 years old. It causes persistent fever and widespread blood vessel inflammation. Its most serious complication is involvement of the coronary arteries, which can lead to aneurysms (balloon-like bulges) and long-term heart problems. With prompt treatment and no cardiac involvement, full recovery is possible.
川崎病是一种急性炎症性疾病，主要影响5岁以下儿童，引起持续发烧及全身血管发炎。最严重的并发症是冠状动脉受累，可导致动脉瘤（血管壁局部膨出）及长期心脏问题。若及时治疗且无心脏受累，可完全康复。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of diagnosis and at what age did it occur?
> 1) 确诊日期及发病年龄？
> 2) Was there any coronary artery involvement or aneurysm? What investigations were done (e.g., ECG, chest X-ray, echocardiogram, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 是否有冠状动脉受累或动脉瘤？曾进行哪些检查（如心电图、胸部X光、超声心动图、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) Is the client currently on medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 客户目前是否在服药？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 4) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 4) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: echocardiogram, ECG, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新报告：超声心动图、心电图、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
> b) Surgical / procedure report (if applicable)
> b) 手术或治疗程序报告（如有）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Admitted to yy Hospital under Dr xx due to Kawasaki disease on dd/mm/yy. Discharged well with no cardiac involvement confirmed. Echocardiogram, chest X-ray and blood tests done — reports attached. Fully recovered since.
> dd/mm/yy 因川崎病住入 yy 医院，由 Dr xx 医生诊治，确认无心脏受累，康复出院。超声心动图、胸部X光及血液检查已完成，报告随附。自此已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if no cardiac involvement and full recovery confirmed.
> - 若无心脏受累且确认完全康复，可标准承保。
> - Decline or rated if coronary artery aneurysm or ongoing cardiac involvement.
> - 若有冠状动脉瘤或持续心脏受累，拒保或额外费率。
