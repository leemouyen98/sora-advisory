#Medical_Underwriting/Cardiovascular_Diseases

A heart attack (myocardial infarction) occurs when blood supply to part of the heart muscle is suddenly cut off, usually due to a blocked coronary artery. Heart muscle begins to die within minutes. It is a medical emergency. A history of heart attack renders an applicant non-insurable across all coverage types.
心脏病发作（心肌梗塞）是指心脏某部分肌肉的供血突然中断，通常由冠状动脉阻塞引起。心肌在数分钟内开始坏死，属于医疗紧急状况。有心脏病发作病史的申请人，所有保险类型均不可承保。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) What is the date of the heart attack and which artery was blocked?
> 1) 心脏病发作日期及哪条动脉阻塞？
> 2) What investigations were done (e.g., angiogram, ECG, cardiac enzymes / troponin, blood tests)? Please provide dates and results.
> 2) 曾进行哪些检查（如血管造影、心电图、心脏酶 / 肌钙蛋白、血液检查）？请提供日期及结果。
> 3) What procedure was performed (e.g., angioplasty, stenting, bypass surgery)? Please state the type, date and outcome.
> 3) 曾接受哪种治疗程序（如血管成形术、支架置入、搭桥手术）？请说明类型、日期及结果。
> 4) Is the client currently on cardiac medication (e.g., aspirin, statin, beta-blocker)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 客户目前是否在服用心脏药物（如阿司匹林、他汀类、β 受体阻滞剂）？如有，请说明药物类型及疗程。
> 5) Is the client still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent?
> 5) 客户是否仍在定期复诊？如有，多久一次？
