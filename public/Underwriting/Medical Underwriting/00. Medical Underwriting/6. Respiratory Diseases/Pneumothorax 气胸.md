#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Pneumothorax is the presence of air in the pleural cavity — the space between the lungs and chest wall. When the lung surface or chest wall is breached, air enters this space and causes the lung to collapse. It may result from a chest injury, excess pressure on the lungs, or an underlying lung disease such as COPD, asthma, cystic fibrosis, or tuberculosis.
气胸是指胸膜腔（肺部与胸壁之间的空间）内存在气体。当肺表面或胸壁出现破损，气体便进入胸膜腔，导致肺部塌陷。气胸可由胸部创伤、肺部压力过高，或慢性阻塞性肺病、哮喘、囊性纤维化、肺结核等潜在肺部疾病引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine, chest X-ray & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：血液、尿液、胸部X光及扫描检查
> b) Surgery report if performed
> b) 如有进行手术，请附上手术报告
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
