#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Emphysema is a chronic lung condition caused by damage to the walls of the air sacs (alveoli) deep inside the lungs. When these thin, fragile walls are destroyed, the lungs lose their elasticity and ability to exchange oxygen efficiently. Cigarette smoking is the most common cause. Emphysema is irreversible and is classified as a form of COPD.
肺气肿是一种慢性肺部疾病，由肺部深处气囊（肺泡）壁受损所致。当这些薄而脆弱的气囊壁被破坏后，肺部失去弹性，无法有效进行气体交换。吸烟是最常见的病因。肺气肿不可逆转，属于慢性阻塞性肺疾病（COPD）的一种。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, ECG, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、心电图、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine, chest X-ray & scan tests
