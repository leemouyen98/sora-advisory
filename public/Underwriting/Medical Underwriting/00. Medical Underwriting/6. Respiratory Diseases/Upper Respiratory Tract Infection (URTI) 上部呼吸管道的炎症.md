#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

An upper respiratory tract infection (URTI) affects the upper part of the respiratory system — the nose, sinuses, and throat. Common symptoms include a runny nose, sore throat, and cough. Treatment usually involves rest, fluids, and over-the-counter pain relievers. Most URTIs resolve on their own within a week or two.
上呼吸道感染影响呼吸系统的上部，包括鼻腔、鼻窦和咽喉。常见症状包括流鼻涕、喉咙痛及咳嗽。治疗通常包括休息、补充水分及服用非处方止痛药。大多数上呼吸道感染可在一至两周内自行痊愈。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: blood, urine, chest X-ray & scan tests

> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
