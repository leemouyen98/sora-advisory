#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

A respiratory condition marked by attacks of spasm in the bronchi of the lungs, causing difficulty in breathing. It is usually connected to allergic reaction or other forms of hypersensitivity.
哮喘是一种以支气管痉挛发作为特征的呼吸系统疾病，导致呼吸困难，通常与过敏反应或其他过敏反应形式有关。

It is one of the most common long-term diseases of children and adults. Asthma causes wheezing, breathlessness, chest tightness, and coughing at night or early in the morning.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such lung function test, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 lung function test, blood & urine tests etc...）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
