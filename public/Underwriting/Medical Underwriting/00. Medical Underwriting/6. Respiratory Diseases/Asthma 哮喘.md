#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition caused by inflammation and spasm of the airways (bronchi) in the lungs, leading to episodes of wheezing, breathlessness, chest tightness, and coughing — often worse at night or in the early morning. It is one of the most common long-term conditions in both children and adults, usually triggered by allergies or other hypersensitivity reactions.
哮喘是一种慢性呼吸系统疾病，由于肺部气道（支气管）发炎和痉挛，导致反复发作的喘鸣、呼吸困难、胸闷及咳嗽，症状常在夜间或清晨加重。哮喘是儿童及成人最常见的慢性疾病之一，通常由过敏反应或其他过敏体质因素所诱发。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
