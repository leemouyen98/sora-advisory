#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Pneumonia is an acute respiratory infection where the air sacs (alveoli) in one or both lungs fill with fluid or pus, causing painful breathing and reduced oxygen intake. In adults, it is most commonly caused by bacteria. Treatment depends on the cause and typically includes antibiotics for bacterial pneumonia, along with cough suppressants and fever reducers as needed.
肺炎是一种急性呼吸道感染，肺部一侧或两侧的气囊（肺泡）充满液体或脓液，导致呼吸疼痛和氧气摄入减少。在成人中，最常见的病因是细菌。治疗方法取决于病因，细菌性肺炎通常使用抗生素，并按需配合止咳药和退烧药。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
