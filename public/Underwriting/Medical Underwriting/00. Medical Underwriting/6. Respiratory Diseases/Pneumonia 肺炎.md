#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Is a form of acute respiratory infection that affects the lungs. The lungs are made up of small sacs called alveoli, which fill with air when a healthy person breathes. When an individual has pneumonia, the alveoli are filled with pus and fluid, which makes breathing painful and limits oxygen intake.

Bacteria are a common cause of pneumonia in adults.

Treatment
1. Antibiotics. These medicines are used to treat bacterial pneumonia.
1. 抗生素：用于治疗细菌性肺炎。
2. Cough medicine. This medicine may be used to calm your cough so that you can rest.
2. 止咳药：用于缓解咳嗽以帮助休息。
3. Fever reducers/pain relievers. You may take these as needed for fever and discomfort.
3. 退烧药/止痛药：可按需服用以缓解发烧和不适。
