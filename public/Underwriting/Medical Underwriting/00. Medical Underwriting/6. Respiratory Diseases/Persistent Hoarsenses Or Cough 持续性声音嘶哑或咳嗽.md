#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Persistent hoarseness or cough that does not resolve within 3 weeks warrants further investigation. Hoarseness is most commonly caused by vocal overuse, vocal cord polyps, or infection. A chronic cough may indicate underlying conditions such as asthma, acid reflux, or — in more serious cases — lung disease.
超过3周未愈的持续性声音嘶哑或咳嗽需进一步检查。声音嘶哑最常见的原因是声带过度使用、声带息肉或感染；持续性咳嗽可能提示哮喘、胃酸反流或肺部疾病等潜在状况。

## HOARSENESS
## 声音嘶哑

The most common causes of chronic hoarseness include overuse of the voice (particularly in teachers, performers, and speakers), persistent bacterial or viral infections, and polyps, cysts, or nodules on the vocal cords.
慢性声音嘶哑最常见的原因包括声带过度使用（尤其是教师、表演者及演说人士）、持续性细菌或病毒感染，以及声带上的息肉、囊肿或结节。

## COUGH
## 咳嗽

A cough is a reflex action to clear the airways of mucus and irritants. Most coughs resolve within 3 weeks without treatment. A cough that persists, or is accompanied by shortness of breath, blood-tinged phlegm, or mucus production, may indicate a more serious underlying condition.
咳嗽是清除气道黏液和刺激物的反射动作，大多数咳嗽在3周内无需治疗可自行消退。若咳嗽持续不愈，或伴随呼吸短促、带血痰液或多痰等症状，则可能提示潜在的较严重疾病。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
