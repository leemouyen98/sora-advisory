#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Bronchitis is infection or inflammation of the bronchi — the main airways that branch off the windpipe and carry air into the lungs. The airways become irritated, swollen, and filled with mucus, resulting in a persistent cough. Acute bronchitis usually clears up within a few weeks; chronic bronchitis requires longer-term management.
支气管炎是支气管（从气管分支进入肺部的主要气道）受到感染或发炎的疾病。气道受刺激后会肿胀并积聚黏液，导致持续性咳嗽。急性支气管炎通常在数周内康复，慢性支气管炎则需要较长期的管理。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
