Is an infection of the main airways of the lungs (bronchi), causing them to become irritated and inflamed. The main airways branch off on either side of your windpipe (trachea). They lead to smaller and smaller airways inside your lungs called bronchioles.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such lung function test, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 5) Fully recovered ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
>
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
