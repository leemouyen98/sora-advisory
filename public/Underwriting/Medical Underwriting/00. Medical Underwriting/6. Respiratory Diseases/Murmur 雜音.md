#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

A heart murmur is a sound heard through a stethoscope caused by turbulent blood flow within the heart or blood vessels. It may be produced by damaged valves, septal defects, narrowed arteries, or abnormal connections between arteries and veins. Murmurs can be innocent (harmless) or indicate an underlying structural heart problem that requires further evaluation.
心脏杂音是借助听诊器可听到的声音，由心脏或血管内湍流血液产生。可由受损瓣膜、间隔缺损、动脉狭窄或动静脉异常交通引起。杂音可以是无害的（功能性杂音），也可能提示需要进一步评估的潜在心脏结构性问题。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as echocardiogram, lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如心脏超声波、肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: ECG（心电图）, echocardiogram（心脏超声波）, chest X-ray, blood & urine tests
> a) 最新相关报告：心电图、心脏超声波、胸部X光、血液及尿液检查
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Murmur was detected during a check-up on dd/mm/yy. Referred to Dr xxx of yy Hospital and admitted for further investigation. Found to have a small hole in the heart (ASD). Discharged well.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 体检时发现心脏杂音，转介至 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生，住院进一步检查，发现有小型心脏缺损（房间隔缺损），康复出院。
> Scanning, blood & urine tests done on dd/mm/yy are attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成的扫描、血液及尿液检查报告随附。
> On regular medical follow-up.
> 定期进行医疗随访。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Postpone until a firm diagnosis / cause is established and full recovery confirmed
> - 延迟至确立明确诊断/病因且完全康复后方可承保
