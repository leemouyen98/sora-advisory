#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

A noise, heard with the aid of stethoscope, that is generated by turbulent blood flow within the heart of blood vessels. Turbulent flow is produced by damaged valves, septal defects, narrowed arteries, or arteriovenous communications.

![[Murmur 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such echocardiogram, lung function, blood & urine tests etc. ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 5) Fully recovered ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any relevant reports such as ECG, blood / urine / chest X-ray, scan tests ?
> 
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Noticed to have murmur during check up on dd/mm/yy, referred to Dr xxx of yy Hospital, admitted for further check up. Found to hvave hole in heart. Discharged well.
> Scanning, blood & urine test done on dd/mm/yy is attached. 
> On medical follow up regularly.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> – Postpone till firm diagnosis / causes establish & completely recovered
