#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

A noise, heard with the aid of stethoscope, that is generated by turbulent blood flow within the heart of blood vessels. Turbulent flow is produced by damaged valves, septal defects, narrowed arteries, or arteriovenous communications.
心脏杂音是借助听诊器可听到的声音，由心脏或血管内湍流血液产生。湍流可由受损瓣膜、间隔缺损、动脉狭窄或动静脉交通引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such echocardiogram, lung function, blood & urine tests etc. ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 echocardiogram, lung function, blood & urine tests etc.）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered ?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

> a) 任何相关报告，如 ECG, blood / urine / chest X-ray, scan tests 。
