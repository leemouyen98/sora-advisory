#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Hyperventilation is abnormally rapid breathing at rest, causing a drop in blood carbon dioxide levels. It is most commonly triggered by anxiety or panic. Symptoms include light-headedness, tingling in the hands or feet, and a rapid heartbeat. It usually resolves when breathing returns to a normal rate.
过度换气是指在静息状态下呼吸异常急促，导致血液中二氧化碳浓度下降，最常见的诱因是焦虑或恐慌。症状包括头晕、手脚麻痹及心跳加速等，通常在呼吸恢复正常频率后可自行缓解。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: ECG（心电图）, blood, urine, chest X-ray & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：心电图、血液、尿液、胸部X光及扫描检查
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of hyperventilation on dd/mm/yy. Seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital and admitted for further investigation. Treated and discharged well.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 有过度换气病史，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，住院进一步检查，治疗后康复出院。
> Blood & urine tests done on dd/mm/yy are attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成的血液及尿液检查报告随附。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。
