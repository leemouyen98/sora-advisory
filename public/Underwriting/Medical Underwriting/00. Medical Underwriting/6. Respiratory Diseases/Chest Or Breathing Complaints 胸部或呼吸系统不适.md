#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Chest or breathing complaints cover a range of symptoms including chest pain, pressure, tightness, and shortness of breath. These may be caused by heart or lung conditions, infections, or anxiety. Any persistent or unexplained chest or breathing symptom should be thoroughly investigated to rule out serious underlying causes before underwriting can proceed.
胸部或呼吸系统不适涵盖多种症状，包括胸痛、胸部压迫感、胸闷及呼吸短促等，可能由心脏或肺部疾病、感染或焦虑等原因引起。任何持续或不明原因的胸部或呼吸症状，均须彻底检查以排除潜在严重病因，方可进行核保评估。

## CHEST COMPLAINTS
## 胸部不适

Pressure, fullness, burning or tightness in the chest. Crushing or searing pain that spreads to the back, neck, jaw, shoulders, and one or both arms. Pain that lasts more than a few minutes, gets worse with activity, goes away and comes back, or varies in intensity. Shortness of breath.
胸部有压迫感、饱胀感、灼热感或紧绷感。剧烈或灼烧样疼痛可放射至背部、颈部、下颌、肩部及一侧或双侧手臂。疼痛持续数分钟以上、活动时加剧、反复出现或程度不一，以及呼吸短促。

## BREATHING COMPLAINTS
## 呼吸系统不适

Breathing discomfort often presents as a sharp pain when breathing, coughing, or sneezing. The most common causes of pleuritic chest pain are bacterial or viral infections, pulmonary embolism, and pneumothorax. Other less common causes include rheumatoid arthritis, lupus, and cancer.
呼吸不适常表现为呼吸、咳嗽或打喷嚏时的刺痛感。胸膜性胸痛最常见的原因是细菌或病毒感染、肺栓塞及气胸，其他较少见的原因包括类风湿性关节炎、红斑狼疮及癌症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, ECG, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、心电图、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest relevant reports: ECG（心电图）, chest X-ray（胸部X光）, blood, urine & scan tests
> a) 最新相关报告：心电图、胸部X光、血液、尿液及扫描检查
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of 1 episode of chest pain on dd/mm/yy. Seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. ECG and chest X-ray were performed — all results normal.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 有1次胸痛发作，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治，心电图及胸部X光检查结果均正常。
> Scanning, blood & urine tests done on dd/mm/yy are attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成的扫描、血液及尿液检查报告随附。
> Fully recovered since.
> 自此已完全康复。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Postpone until a firm diagnosis / cause is established and full recovery confirmed
> - 延迟至确立明确诊断/病因且完全康复后方可承保
