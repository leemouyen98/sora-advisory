#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Pulmonary oedema is the accumulation of excessive fluid in the air sacs (alveoli) and lung tissue, impairing the ability to exchange oxygen. It can be life-threatening in severe cases and requires urgent management. The most common cause is congestive heart failure — when the heart cannot pump efficiently, fluid backs up into the pulmonary veins and is forced into the lungs.
肺水肿是肺部气囊（肺泡）及肺组织中液体过度积聚的疾病，影响氧气交换。严重情况下可危及生命，需紧急处理。最常见的病因是充血性心力衰竭——当心脏无法有效泵血时，血液积聚在肺静脉，液体被压入肺部气囊。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, lung function test, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、肺功能测试、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
