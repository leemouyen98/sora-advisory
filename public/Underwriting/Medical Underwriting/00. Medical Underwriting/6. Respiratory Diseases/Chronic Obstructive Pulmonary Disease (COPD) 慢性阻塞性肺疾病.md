#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Refers to a group of diseases that cause airflow blockage and breathing- related problems. It includes emphysema and chronic bronchitis. Smoking is the main cause of COPD.

![[ Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such chest X-ray, lung function, ECG, blood & urine tests etc.. ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 5) Fully recovered ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / chest X-ray, scan tests ?
>
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have COPD on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. On regular medical follow up currently.
