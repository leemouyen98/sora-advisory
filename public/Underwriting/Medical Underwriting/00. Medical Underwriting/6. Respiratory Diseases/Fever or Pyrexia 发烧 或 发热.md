#Medical_Underwriting/Respiratory_Diseases

Fever (pyrexia) is a rise in body temperature above normal. For clinical purposes, a patient is considered febrile if oral temperature exceeds 37.5°C (99.5°F) or rectal temperature exceeds 38°C (100.5°F). A fever is usually a sign of the body fighting an infection. Investigations typically include a full blood count, urine test, and chest X-ray to identify the cause.
发烧（发热）是指体温高于正常水平。从临床角度，口腔温度超过37.5°C（99.5°F）或直肠温度超过38°C（100.5°F），即视为发烧。发烧通常是身体对抗感染的信号，检查一般包括全血细胞计数、尿液检查及胸部X光，以确定病因。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual date of diagnosis?
> 1) 诊断的实际日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as chest X-ray, blood & urine tests? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过相关检查（如胸部X光、血液及尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment? If yes, what type & period of treatment?
> 3) 是否在服药或接受治疗？如是，请说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow-up? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc)?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
> 5) Fully recovered?
> 5) 是否已完全康复？
