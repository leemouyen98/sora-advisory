#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A state of unarousable unconsciousness. An individual or deep state of coma is alive but unable to move or respond to his or her environment. [Glasgow coma scale](https://www.quickguide.com.my/3-brain-nervious-diseases/Hyperlink%20-%20Coma/Glasscoc%20coma%20chart.ppt) is a numerical system used to estimate a patient’s level of consciousness (coma) after head injury.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such CT scan / MRI, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) Any surgery has been performed ? If yes, please state the date & type of operation.
> 4) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 5) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ? b) Surgery / removal report if performed.
>
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Coma due to accident on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Investigation tests done & revealed was due to head injury. Discharged well with 14 days admission.
> CT scan, x-ray, blood & urine test were done on dd/mm/yy. Reports are attached here.
> Currently on medical follow up.
