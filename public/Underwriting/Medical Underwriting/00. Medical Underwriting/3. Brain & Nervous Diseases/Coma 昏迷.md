#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A coma is a state of deep, prolonged unconsciousness where a person is alive but unable to respond to their environment, voice, or pain. It is not a disease itself but a symptom of an underlying condition — such as severe brain injury, stroke, poisoning, or metabolic disturbance. The Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS) is used to assess the depth of coma after head injury.
昏迷是一种深度、持续性的意识丧失状态，患者虽存活但无法对外界环境、声音或疼痛作出反应。昏迷本身不是疾病，而是潜在病症（如严重脑损伤、中风、中毒或代谢紊乱）的症状。格拉斯哥昏迷量表（GCS）用于评估头部受伤后的昏迷深度。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When did it occur, and what was the cause?
> 1) 何时发生？原因是什么？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术报告
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Coma due to road accident on dd/mm/yy. Treated by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Admitted for 14 days.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 因交通意外陷入昏迷，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。住院14天。
> Investigation results showed head injury as cause. Discharged well.
> 检查显示为头部受伤所致，康复出院。
> CT scan, X-ray, blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached. Currently on follow-up.
> dd/mm/yy 完成CT扫描、X光、血液及尿液检查，报告随附。目前仍在随访。

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Decline if brain damage or complications are present
> - 若有脑损伤或并发症，拒保
