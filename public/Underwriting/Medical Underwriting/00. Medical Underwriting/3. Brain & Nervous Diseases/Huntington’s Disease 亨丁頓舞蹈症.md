#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Huntington's disease is a progressive, inherited neurological disorder caused by a faulty gene that gradually destroys nerve cells in the brain. It causes a wide range of physical and psychological symptoms — including uncontrolled movements (chorea), cognitive decline, and psychiatric changes. Symptoms typically begin between the ages of 30 and 50, and the disease is usually fatal within 10–20 years of onset. There is currently no cure.
亨廷顿病是一种进行性遗传性神经系统疾病，由一个缺陷基因逐渐破坏大脑神经细胞引起。可引起广泛的身体和心理症状，包括不受控制的运动（舞蹈病）、认知能力下降及精神变化。症状通常在30至50岁之间出现，发病后通常在10至20年内导致死亡。目前尚无根治方法。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and by which doctor / hospital?
> 1) 何时确诊？由哪位医生 / 哪家医院诊断？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. genetic test, CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如基因检测、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: genetic test（基因检测）, CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：基因检测、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
