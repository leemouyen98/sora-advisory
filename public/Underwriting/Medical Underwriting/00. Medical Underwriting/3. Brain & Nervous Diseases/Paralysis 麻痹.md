#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Paralysis is the loss of muscle function in one or more parts of the body, resulting from damage to the nervous system. It can be complete (total loss of movement) or partial, and may affect one side (hemiplegia), both legs (paraplegia), or all four limbs (quadriplegia). Causes include stroke, spinal cord injury, nerve damage, or neurological diseases.
麻痹是指一个或多个身体部位因神经系统损伤而丧失肌肉功能。可以是完全性（完全丧失运动能力）或部分性，影响范围可为单侧（偏瘫）、双腿（截瘫）或四肢（四肢瘫痪）。原因包括中风、脊髓损伤、神经损伤或神经系统疾病。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what was the cause (e.g. stroke, accident, disease)?
> 1) 何时确诊？原因是什么（如中风、意外、疾病）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, X-ray, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、X光、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. physiotherapy, surgery)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如物理治疗、手术）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## 所需文件
