#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Alzheimer's disease is the most common form of dementia, accounting for 60–80% of cases. It is a progressive brain disorder that gradually destroys memory, thinking skills, and eventually the ability to carry out simple daily tasks. It primarily affects parts of the brain responsible for thought, memory, and language.
阿尔茨海默病是最常见的痴呆症，占所有病例的60至80%。这是一种进行性脑部疾病，逐渐损害记忆、思维能力，最终影响日常生活自理能力。它主要影响大脑中负责思考、记忆和语言的区域。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and by which doctor / hospital?
> 1) 何时确诊？由哪位医生 / 哪家医院诊断？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT scan, MRI, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT扫描、MRI、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT scan（CT扫描）, MRI（磁力共振）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT扫描、MRI、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
