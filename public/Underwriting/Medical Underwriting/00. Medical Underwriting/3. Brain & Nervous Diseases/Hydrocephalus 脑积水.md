#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Hydrocephalus is an abnormal build-up of cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) in the ventricles (fluid-filled cavities) of the brain. The excess fluid increases pressure on the brain and can damage brain tissue. It can occur at any age but is most common in infants and older adults. Treatment typically involves a surgical shunt to drain excess fluid.
脑积水是指脑室（脑内充满液体的腔隙）中脑脊液（CSF）异常积聚。过多的液体会增加脑部压力，可能损伤脑组织。可发生于任何年龄，但在婴儿和老年人中最为常见。治疗通常需要手术植入分流管，以引流多余的脑脊液。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what was the cause (e.g. congenital, post-infection, tumour)?
> 1) 何时确诊？原因是什么（如先天性、感染后、肿瘤引起）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was a shunt or surgery performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过分流管手术或其他手术？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
