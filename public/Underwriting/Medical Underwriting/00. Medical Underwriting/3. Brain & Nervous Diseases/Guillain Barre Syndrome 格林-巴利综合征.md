#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Guillain-Barré syndrome (GBS) is a rare autoimmune disease affecting the peripheral nervous system, where the body's immune system mistakenly attacks the nerves. It typically develops 10–12 days after a respiratory or gastrointestinal infection, causing progressive limb weakness and numbness. Most patients recover well with treatment, although recovery can be prolonged.
格林-巴利综合征（GBS）是一种罕见的自身免疫性疾病，免疫系统错误地攻击周围神经系统。通常在呼吸道或胃肠道感染后10至12天发病，出现四肢进行性无力和麻木。大多数患者经治疗后恢复良好，但康复过程可能较漫长。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and how severe was it (e.g. required ventilator support)?
> 1) 何时确诊？严重程度如何（如是否需要呼吸机支持）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. nerve conduction study, lumbar puncture, MRI, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如神经传导检查、腰椎穿刺、MRI、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. IVIG, plasmapheresis, physiotherapy)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如免疫球蛋白、血浆置换、物理治疗）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: nerve conduction study（神经传导检查）, MRI（磁力共振）, blood tests
> a) 最新报告：神经传导检查、MRI、血液检查

> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
