#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A brain haemorrhage is bleeding in or around the brain, caused by a ruptured artery or a significant blow to the head. The bleeding kills brain cells and can cause lasting neurological damage. A brain injury refers to damage resulting from trauma, stroke, or oxygen deprivation. Both are medical emergencies requiring immediate treatment.
脑出血是指脑部或脑部周围出血，由动脉破裂或头部受到严重撞击引起。出血会导致脑细胞死亡，可造成持久的神经功能损伤。脑损伤是指由创伤、中风或缺氧引起的脑部损害。两者均属需要立即处理的医疗紧急状况。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what was the cause (e.g. trauma, spontaneous rupture)?
> 1) 何时确诊？原因是什么（如创伤、自发性破裂）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT scan, MRI, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT扫描、MRI、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术？如是，请说明日期及手术类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT scan（CT扫描）, MRI（磁力共振）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT扫描、MRI、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术报告
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with brain injury with bleeding on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Admitted for 15 days.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有脑损伤伴出血。住院15天。
> Surgery performed on dd/mm/yy to remove bleeding and clot.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 进行手术，清除出血及血块。
> CT scan, ECG, blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached. Monthly follow-up required.
> dd/mm/yy 完成CT扫描、心电图、血液及尿液检查，报告随附。需每月进行随访。
