#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Heat stroke is a life-threatening condition where the body's temperature regulation mechanism fails, causing core body temperature to rise to 40°C (106°F) or higher within minutes. It is characterised by elevated body temperature, absence of sweating, confusion, and possible loss of consciousness. Immediate cooling — especially cold water immersion — is the most effective emergency treatment.
中暑是一种危及生命的状况，当身体的体温调节机制失效，核心体温在短时间内升至40°C（106°F）或以上。特征包括体温升高、无汗、意识混乱及可能失去意识。立即降温——尤其是冷水浸泡——是最有效的紧急处理方式。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When did it occur, and what was the situation (e.g. outdoor work, sports)?
> 1) 何时发生？当时的情况（如户外工作、运动）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. blood/urine tests, CT scan)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如血液/尿液检查、CT扫描）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: blood/urine tests, admission/discharge summary if hospitalised
> a) 最新报告：血液/尿液检查，如有住院则附上入院/出院摘要
>
> MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of 1 episode of heat stroke on dd/mm/yy. Treated by Dr xxx of yy Clinic. Treatment given and fully recovered.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 有1次中暑发作，由 yy 诊所 Dr xxx 医生诊治。经治疗后已完全康复。
