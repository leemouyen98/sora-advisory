#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A convulsion (fit or seizure) is an involuntary contraction of the muscles causing the body and limbs to jerk or contort. Seizures can be caused by fever, head injury, infection, epilepsy, or other neurological conditions. A single isolated seizure with a known cause (such as high fever) generally carries a better prognosis than recurrent unexplained seizures.
痉挛（抽搐或发作）是肌肉不自主收缩，导致身体和四肢抖动或扭曲的状况。可由发烧、头部受伤、感染、癫痫或其他神经系统疾病引起。由已知原因（如高烧）引起的单次孤立性发作，预后通常优于反复发作且原因不明的情况。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When did it occur, and what was the cause (e.g. fever, epilepsy, head injury)?
> 1) 何时发生？原因是什么（如发烧、癫痫、头部受伤）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. EEG, CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如脑电图、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: EEG（脑电图）, CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：脑电图、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of 1 episode of convulsion (30 seconds) on dd/mm/yy. Treated by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 有1次持续30秒的抽搐发作，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。
> Investigation confirmed fever as cause. Admitted for 1 day, discharged well. No recurrence since.
> 检查确认为发烧所致。住院1天，康复出院。此后无复发。
> Blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。
