#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Motor neuron disease (MND) is a progressive neurological condition affecting the motor neurons — the nerve cells in the brain and spinal cord that control muscle movement. As motor neurons degenerate and die, muscles gradually weaken, waste away, and stop working. There is currently no cure. Treatment focuses on managing symptoms and maintaining quality of life. The most well-known form is ALS (amyotrophic lateral sclerosis).
运动神经元病（MND）是一种进行性神经系统疾病，影响运动神经元——大脑和脊髓中控制肌肉运动的神经细胞。随着运动神经元退化和死亡，肌肉逐渐无力、萎缩并停止运作。目前尚无根治方法，治疗以控制症状和维持生活质量为主。最广为人知的类型是肌萎缩侧索硬化症（ALS）。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. ALS, PLS, PMA)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如ALS、PLS、PMA）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. electromyography/EMG, MRI, nerve conduction study, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如肌电图/EMG、MRI、神经传导检查、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## 所需文件

> a) 最新报告：肌电图、MRI、神经传导检查、血液检查

> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
