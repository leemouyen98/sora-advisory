#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Prolonged or recurrent headache refers to persistent pain felt deep within the skull. Most headaches are caused by tension, fatigue, or stress, but some may indicate serious intracranial conditions. Any recurrent or severe headache should be investigated to rule out underlying causes.
长期反复头痛是指持续出现于颅骨深处的疼痛。大多数头痛由紧张、疲劳或压力引起，但部分可能提示严重的颅内病变。任何反复发作或剧烈的头痛均应进行检查，以排除潜在病因。

Prolonged or recurrent dizziness describes a sensation of feeling faint, unsteady, or as if the surroundings are spinning (vertigo). It is one of the most common reasons adults visit a doctor. Causes range from benign vestibular dysfunction to more serious cardiovascular or neurological conditions.
长期反复头晕是指感觉虚弱、站立不稳或周围环境旋转（眩晕）的状态。这是成人就医最常见的原因之一。原因从良性的前庭功能障碍到较严重的心血管或神经系统疾病不等。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When did it start, and how often do episodes occur?
> 1) 何时开始？发作频率如何？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
