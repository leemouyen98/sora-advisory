#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Bacterial meningitis is a serious infection causing inflammation of the meninges — the protective membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord. It occurs when bacteria from another infection elsewhere in the body travel through the bloodstream to the brain. Untreated, it can rapidly become life-threatening and may lead to long-term complications such as hearing loss, brain damage, or paralysis.
细菌性脑膜炎是一种严重的感染，导致包覆脑部和脊髓的脑膜发炎。细菌通常从身体其他部位的感染经血液扩散至脑部。若不及时治疗，可迅速危及生命，并可能导致听力丧失、脑损伤或瘫痪等长期并发症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and by which doctor / hospital?
> 1) 何时确诊？由哪位医生 / 哪家医院诊断？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. lumbar puncture, CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如腰椎穿刺、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. antibiotics, steroids)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如抗生素、类固醇）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: lumbar puncture（腰椎穿刺）, CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：腰椎穿刺、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
