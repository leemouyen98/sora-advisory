#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Encephalitis is inflammation of the brain, most commonly caused by a viral infection. In many cases it produces only mild flu-like symptoms such as fever or headache, or no symptoms at all. However, severe cases can cause confusion, seizures, paralysis, or loss of consciousness, and may require hospitalisation and intensive treatment.
脑炎是脑部的炎症，最常见的原因是病毒感染。许多情况下仅引起轻微的流感样症状，如发烧或头痛，甚至毫无症状。然而，严重病例可导致意识混乱、癫痫发作、瘫痪或意识丧失，可能需要住院及密集治疗。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. viral, autoimmune)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如病毒性、自身免疫性）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, lumbar puncture, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、腰椎穿刺、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. antiviral, steroids)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如抗病毒药物、类固醇）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
