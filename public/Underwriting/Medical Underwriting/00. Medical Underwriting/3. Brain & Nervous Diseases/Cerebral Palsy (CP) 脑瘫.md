#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Cerebral palsy (CP) is the most common motor disability in childhood. It is a group of disorders affecting movement, balance, and posture, caused by abnormal brain development or damage to the developing brain — most often occurring before or during birth. The type and severity of movement problems vary widely from person to person.
脑性瘫痪（脑瘫）是儿童最常见的运动障碍。这是一组影响运动、平衡和姿势的疾病，由脑部发育异常或发育中的大脑受损引起——通常发生于出生前或出生时。运动问题的类型和严重程度因人而异。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type of CP (e.g. spastic, dyskinetic, ataxic)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型的脑瘫（如痉挛型、运动障碍型、共济失调型）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. physiotherapy, medication, surgery)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如物理治疗、药物、手术）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## 所需文件

> a) 最新报告：CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查

> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
