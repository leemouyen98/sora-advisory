#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Parkinson's disease is a progressive neurological disorder affecting movement. It is caused by the loss of dopamine-producing nerve cells in the brain. Common symptoms include resting tremor, slowness of movement (bradykinesia), muscle stiffness, and balance problems. There is no cure, but most patients can maintain a good quality of life with medication — particularly levodopa.
帕金森症是一种影响运动功能的进行性神经系统疾病，由脑内产生多巴胺的神经细胞减少引起。常见症状包括静止性震颤、动作迟缓（运动减少症）、肌肉僵硬及平衡问题。目前无法根治，但大多数患者通过药物治疗（尤其是左旋多巴）可维持较好的生活质量。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and at what stage?
> 1) 何时确诊？处于哪个阶段？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, DaTscan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、多巴胺转运体扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
