#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A transient ischaemic attack (TIA) is a temporary episode of stroke-like symptoms lasting only a few minutes, caused by a brief interruption of blood flow to the brain. Unlike a full stroke, a TIA does not cause permanent brain damage. However, it is a serious warning sign — people who have had a TIA are at significantly higher risk of having a major stroke in the days or weeks that follow.
短暂性脑缺血发作（TIA）是由脑部血流短暂中断引起的类中风症状发作，通常仅持续数分钟。与完全性中风不同，TIA不会造成永久性脑损伤。但这是一个严重的警示信号——发生过TIA的人在随后数天或数周内发生重大中风的风险显著升高。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and how long did the episode last?
> 1) 何时确诊？发作持续多长时间？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, ECG, carotid ultrasound, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、心电图、颈动脉超声波、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery or procedure performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术或治疗？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, ECG（心电图）, carotid ultrasound（颈动脉超声波）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT/MRI扫描、心电图、颈动脉超声波、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术报告

> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed with TIA on dd/mm/yy by Dr xxx of yy Hospital. Admitted for 1 day only.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断为短暂性脑缺血发作（TIA）。住院仅1天。
> History of hypertension since mm/yy — on medication with quarterly follow-up.
> 自 mm/yy 起有高血压病史，服药并每季度随访。
> CT scan, ECG, blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached. Annual follow-up required.
> dd/mm/yy 完成CT扫描、心电图、血液及尿液检查，报告随附。需每年进行随访。
