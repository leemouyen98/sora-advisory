#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Poliomyelitis (polio) is a contagious viral disease that can infect the spinal cord and cause permanent paralysis. It spreads through contaminated water or direct contact with an infected person. While largely eradicated in most countries through vaccination, residual paralysis or weakness from childhood polio may still present as a pre-existing condition in older applicants.
脊髓灰质炎（小儿麻痹症）是一种传染性病毒性疾病，可感染脊髓并导致永久性瘫痪。通过受污染的水或与感染者直接接触传播。虽然大多数国家通过疫苗接种已基本消除此病，但儿童期患病留下的残余瘫痪或虚弱，在年龄较大的申请人中仍可能作为既往病症出现。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and which limb(s) were affected?
> 1) 何时确诊？哪些肢体受到影响？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. nerve tests, X-ray, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如神经检查、X光、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or surgery? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或手术？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
