#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A disorder of brain function characterised by recurrent seizures that have a sudden onset.

Is a common condition that affects the brain and causes frequent seizures. Seizures are bursts of electrical activity in the brain that temporarily affect how it works. They can cause a wide range of symptoms. Epilepsy can start at any age, but usually starts either in childhood or in people over 60.

Although epilepsy can’t be cured, many treatment options are available. Up to 70% of people with epilepsy can manage the disease with medications.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2) Any investigation tests such CT scan / MRI, blood & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1) From client / doctor
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
>
> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor
>
> 3) Epilepsy Questionnaire from Applicant & attending doctor

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have epilepsy on dd/mm/yy, seen by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> CT scan, blood & urine test were done on dd/mm/yy. Reports are attached here.
> Currently on medication & medical follow up.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES

> Life / TPD:
> – Depends on the outcomes of examination/reports.
> – Sub-standard case provided good controlled of case
>
> CI / Medical insurance:
> – Can be standard case provided completed treatment & no attack more than 5 years
> – Otherwise, exclusion / decline on epilepsy (depending on number of attack & duration)
