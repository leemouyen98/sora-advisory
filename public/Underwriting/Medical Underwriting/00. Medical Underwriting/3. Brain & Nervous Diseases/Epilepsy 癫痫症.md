#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Epilepsy is a neurological condition characterised by recurrent, sudden-onset seizures caused by abnormal electrical activity in the brain. It can affect people of any age, though it most commonly begins in childhood or in adults over 60. While there is no cure, up to 70% of people with epilepsy can achieve good seizure control with medication.
癫痫是一种以反复、突发性癫痫发作为特征的神经系统疾病，由大脑异常放电引起。可影响任何年龄段，但最常在儿童期或60岁以上的成人中开始发病。虽然目前无法根治，但约70%的患者可通过药物有效控制发作。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type of epilepsy?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型的癫痫？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. EEG, CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如脑电图、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: EEG（脑电图）, CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：脑电图、CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
>
> Epilepsy Questionnaire completed by applicant and treating doctor
> 申请人及主诊医生填写癫痫问卷

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD:
> 人寿 / 全残：
> - Depends on investigation results — typically sub-standard if well controlled
> - 视检查结果而定，病情控制良好者通常次标准承保
>
> CI / Medical:
> 严重疾病 / 医疗：
> - Standard if treatment completed and no attacks for more than 5 years
> - 完成治疗且5年以上无复发者，可标准承保
> - Otherwise exclusion or decline depending on frequency and duration of attacks
> - 否则视发作频率及持续时间，除外承保或拒保
