#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Unconsciousness is a state where a person is unresponsive to their environment, unable to react to voices, sounds, or physical touch. It differs from normal sleep in that the person cannot be awakened. Causes include epilepsy, head trauma, stroke, severe low blood sugar, drug overdose, or cardiac events. Brief unconsciousness (syncope) may be benign, while prolonged unconsciousness typically indicates a serious underlying condition.
无意识是指一种对外界环境完全无反应的状态，无法对声音、呼唤或身体接触作出反应。与正常睡眠不同，患者无法被唤醒。原因包括癫痫、头部创伤、中风、严重低血糖、药物过量或心脏事件。短暂性意识丧失（晕厥）可能是良性的，而长时间意识丧失通常提示严重的潜在病症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When did it occur, and what was the cause?
> 1) 何时发生？原因是什么？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, EEG, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、脑电图、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: CT/MRI scan（CT/MRI扫描）, EEG（脑电图）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：CT/MRI扫描、脑电图、血液/尿液检查
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> History of 1 episode of unconsciousness due to epilepsy on dd/mm/yy. Admitted and treated by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 因癫痫有1次意识丧失发作，住院并由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。
> Investigation results normal. Admitted for 1 day, discharged well. Blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached.
> 检查结果正常。住院1天，康复出院。dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。
> Currently on anti-epileptic medication.
> 目前在服用抗癫痫药物。
