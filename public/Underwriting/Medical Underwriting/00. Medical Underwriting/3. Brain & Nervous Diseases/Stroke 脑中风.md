#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

A stroke occurs when blood supply to part of the brain is suddenly interrupted — either by a blocked artery (ischaemic stroke, ~85% of cases) or a burst blood vessel (haemorrhagic stroke). Without blood and oxygen, brain cells begin dying within minutes. Stroke is a medical emergency requiring immediate treatment. Early action can minimise brain damage and long-term disability.
中风发生于大脑某部分的血液供应突然中断——原因是动脉堵塞（缺血性中风，约占85%）或血管破裂（出血性中风）。缺乏血液和氧气，脑细胞在数分钟内开始死亡。中风是需要立即处理的医疗紧急状况，及早治疗可减少脑损伤及长期残障。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (ischaemic or haemorrhagic)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（缺血性或出血性）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, ECG, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、心电图、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery or procedure performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术或治疗？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
