#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Myelitis is inflammation of the spinal cord that disrupts the normal flow of nerve signals between the brain and the rest of the body. It can cause pain, weakness, paralysis, bladder/bowel dysfunction, and sensory changes. Causes include viral infections, autoimmune conditions (such as MS), and bacterial infections. Transverse myelitis — where inflammation affects the entire width of the spinal cord — is the most common form.
脊髓炎是脊髓的炎症，会干扰大脑与身体其他部位之间正常的神经信号传导。可引起疼痛、无力、瘫痪、膀胱/肠道功能障碍及感觉异常。原因包括病毒感染、自身免疫性疾病（如多发性硬化症）及细菌感染。横贯性脊髓炎——炎症影响脊髓整个横截面——是最常见的类型。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. transverse myelitis, viral myelitis)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如横贯性脊髓炎、病毒性脊髓炎）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. MRI spine, lumbar puncture, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如脊髓MRI、腰椎穿刺、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (e.g. steroids, IVIG, physiotherapy)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（如类固醇、免疫球蛋白、物理治疗）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
