#Medical_Underwriting/Brain_Nervous_Diseases

Migraine is a common neurological condition affecting about 1 in 5 women and 1 in 15 men. It typically causes moderate to severe throbbing or pulsating pain on one side of the head, often accompanied by nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light and sound. Some migraines are preceded by an "aura" — visual disturbances or tingling sensations. The exact cause is unknown, but it is thought to involve abnormal brain activity.
偏头痛是一种常见的神经系统疾病，约影响五分之一的女性和十五分之一的男性。通常引起单侧头部中度至重度的搏动性疼痛，常伴有恶心、呕吐及对光线和声音敏感。部分偏头痛发作前有"先兆"——视觉障碍或刺痛感。确切病因尚不明确，但被认为与大脑异常活动有关。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and how often do attacks occur?
> 1) 何时确诊？发作频率如何？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. CT/MRI scan, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如CT/MRI扫描、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
