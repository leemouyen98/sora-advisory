#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Glycosuria (sugar in the urine) occurs when glucose passes through the kidneys into the urine, which normally should not happen. It is most commonly caused by elevated blood sugar levels — a sign of diabetes — or by kidney damage (renal glycosuria), where the kidneys fail to reabsorb glucose even when blood sugar is normal.
尿糖是指葡萄糖经肾脏进入尿液，这在正常情况下不应发生。最常见的原因是血糖升高——这是糖尿病的信号——或是肾脏损伤（肾性尿糖），即肾脏在血糖正常的情况下也无法重新吸收葡萄糖。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it first detected, and what was the cause (e.g. high blood sugar, kidney-related)?
> 1) 何时首次发现？原因是什么（如血糖偏高、肾脏问题）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. fasting blood sugar/FBS, HbA1c, urine FEME, blood tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如空腹血糖/FBS、糖化血红蛋白/HbA1c、尿液常规检查、血液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: fasting blood sugar（空腹血糖）, HbA1c（糖化血红蛋白）, urine FEME (2 samples)（尿液常规检查，2份）
> a) 最新报告：空腹血糖、糖化血红蛋白、尿液常规检查（2份）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Sugar detected in urine during routine medical check-up on dd/mm/yy. Reviewed by Dr xxx of yy Hospital.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 例行体检发现尿液中含有糖分，由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊治。
> Fasting blood sugar (FBS) and HbA1c tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached.
> dd/mm/yy 完成空腹血糖及糖化血红蛋白检查，报告随附。
> No medication required. Advised to exercise regularly and monitor diet.
> 无需药物，建议定期运动并注意饮食。
