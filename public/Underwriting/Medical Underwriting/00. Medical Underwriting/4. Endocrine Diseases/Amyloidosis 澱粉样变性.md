#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Amyloidosis is a rare disease caused by the abnormal build-up of a protein called amyloid in organs and tissues. This build-up impairs organ function. Commonly affected organs include the heart, kidneys, liver, spleen, nervous system, and digestive tract.
淀粉样变性是一种罕见疾病，由异常蛋白质（淀粉样蛋白）在器官和组织中异常积聚引起，导致器官功能受损。常受影响的器官包括心脏、肾脏、肝脏、脾脏、神经系统及消化道。

It is caused by abnormal plasma cells in the bone marrow producing abnormal light chain proteins, which enter the bloodstream and form amyloid deposits. There is no cure, but treatment can help manage symptoms and slow further amyloid production.
病因是骨髓中异常浆细胞产生异常轻链蛋白，进入血液后形成淀粉样沉积物。目前无法根治，但治疗可帮助控制症状并减缓淀粉样蛋白的进一步产生。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. AL, AA, hereditary)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如AL型、AA型、遗传型）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. biopsy, organ function tests, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如活检、器官功能检查、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
