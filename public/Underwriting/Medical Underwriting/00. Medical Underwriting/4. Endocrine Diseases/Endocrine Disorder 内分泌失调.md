#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Are diseases related to the endocrine glands of the body. The endocrine system produces hormones, which are chemical signals sent out, or secreted, through the bloodstream.

![[Endocrine Disorder 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such blood(Thyroid Function Test) & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 blood(Thyroid Function Test) & urine tests etc...）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any Surgery / removal has been performed ?
> 3) 是否进行过任何手术/切除？
> 4) Any HPE / biopsy test been done ? if yes, please provide the report.
> 4) 是否进行过病理/活检检查？如是，请提供报告。
> 5) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 5) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 6) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 6) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / scan test ?
> a) 任何相关报告，如 blood / urine / scan test 。
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed.
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告。
> c) Thyroid Questionnaire by applicant
> c) 申请人填写甲状腺问卷

> 2) Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS) from attending doctor
