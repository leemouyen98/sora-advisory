#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Endocrine disorders are conditions affecting the endocrine glands — the organs that produce and release hormones directly into the bloodstream to regulate body functions. Examples include thyroid disorders, adrenal disorders, pituitary disorders, and parathyroid conditions. Symptoms vary widely depending on which gland and hormone are affected.
内分泌失调是影响内分泌腺体的疾病——这些腺体直接将激素分泌入血液以调节身体功能。常见例子包括甲状腺疾病、肾上腺疾病、脑垂体疾病及甲状旁腺疾病。症状因受影响的腺体和激素不同而差异很大。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what specific endocrine disorder?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种内分泌失调？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. thyroid function test/TFT, hormone levels, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如甲状腺功能检查/TFT、激素水平、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery or removal performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术或切除？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Were any HPE / biopsy tests done? If yes, state dates and results.
> 4) 是否进行过病理/活检检查？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 5) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 5) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 6) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 6) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: thyroid function test（甲状腺功能检查）, hormone levels（激素水平）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：甲状腺功能检查、激素水平、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
> c) Thyroid Questionnaire completed by applicant (where applicable)
> c) 申请人填写甲状腺问卷（如适用）
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
