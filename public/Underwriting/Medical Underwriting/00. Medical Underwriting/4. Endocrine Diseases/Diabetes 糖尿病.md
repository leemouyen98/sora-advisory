#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Is a chronic (long-lasting) health condition that affects how your body turns food into energy. Most of the food you eat is broken down into sugar (also called glucose) and released into your bloodstream. When your blood sugar goes up, it signals your pancreas to release insulin.
糖尿病是一种影响身体将食物转化为能量方式的慢性（长期）健康状况。大部分食物分解为糖（葡萄糖）并释放入血液。血糖升高时，会向胰腺发出信号以释放胰岛素。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such blood(FBS & HbA1c) & urine tests etc... ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如 blood(FBS & HbA1c) & urine tests etc...）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment (oral medication / insulin therapy) ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗（口服药/胰岛素治疗）？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 一般所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
>
> a) Any relevant reports such as blood (FBS & HbA1c) / urine / scan tests ?
> a) 任何相关报告，如血液（空腹血糖及糖化血红蛋白）/尿液/扫描检查？
>
> b) Diabetes Questionnaire from attending Doctor (on insulin therapy) 2) Medical Examination (on oral medication)
> b) 主治医生填写糖尿病问卷（适用于胰岛素治疗者）2) 体格检查（适用于口服药物者）

## 披露示例

> dd/mm/yy 完成血液（空腹血糖及糖化血红蛋白）及尿液检查，报告随附。

> 目前在服药并定期随访。
