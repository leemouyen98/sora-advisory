#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body either cannot produce enough insulin (Type 1) or cannot use insulin effectively (Type 2), causing blood glucose levels to remain persistently elevated. Over time, uncontrolled diabetes damages blood vessels and nerves, leading to serious complications such as heart disease, stroke, kidney failure, blindness, and limb amputation.
糖尿病是一种慢性疾病，患者体内胰岛素分泌不足（1型）或无法有效利用胰岛素（2型），导致血糖持续偏高。若长期控制不佳，会损伤血管和神经，引发心脏病、中风、肾衰竭、失明及截肢等严重并发症。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (Type 1, Type 2, or gestational)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（1型、2型或妊娠期糖尿病）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. fasting blood sugar/FBS, HbA1c, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如空腹血糖/FBS、糖化血红蛋白/HbA1c、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment (oral medication or insulin)? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗（口服药物或胰岛素）？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: fasting blood sugar（空腹血糖）, HbA1c（糖化血红蛋白）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：空腹血糖、糖化血红蛋白、血液/尿液检查
>
> Diabetes Questionnaire from treating doctor (for insulin therapy cases)
> 主诊医生填写糖尿病问卷（适用于胰岛素治疗者）
>
> Medical examination (for oral medication cases)
> 体格检查（适用于口服药物者）
