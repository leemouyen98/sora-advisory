#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Gout is a common form of inflammatory arthritis caused by a build-up of uric acid in the blood. When uric acid levels are too high — due to overproduction or insufficient excretion by the kidneys — it forms tiny sharp crystals in and around joints, causing sudden, severe pain, swelling, and redness. It most often affects the big toe joint. Attacks come and go, with symptom-free periods in between.
痛风是一种常见的炎性关节炎，由血液中尿酸积聚过多引起。当尿酸水平过高——因生成过多或肾脏排泄不足——会在关节内外形成微小的尖锐结晶，导致突发性剧烈疼痛、肿胀和发红。最常影响大脚趾关节。症状会反复发作，发作间期可无症状。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and which joint(s) are affected?
> 1) 何时确诊？哪些关节受影响？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. uric acid level, blood/urine tests, joint X-ray)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如尿酸水平、血液/尿液检查、关节X光）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 3) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 4) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 4) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: uric acid level（尿酸水平）, blood/urine tests, joint X-ray（关节X光）
> a) 最新报告：尿酸水平、血液/尿液检查、关节X光
> b) Gout Questionnaire completed by applicant and treating doctor
> b) 申请人及主诊医生填写痛风问卷
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES
## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病：
> - Standard if investigation results are normal
> - 检查结果正常者，标准承保
>
> Medical:
> 医疗：
> - Standard if asymptomatic (no joint disability)
> - 无症状（关节无残障）者，标准承保
> - Exclusion if symptomatic or with joint complications
> - 有症状或关节并发症者，除外承保
