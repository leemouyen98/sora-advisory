#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

A goitre is an abnormal enlargement of the thyroid gland, causing a visible lump at the front of the neck that moves up and down when swallowing. The thyroid is a small butterfly-shaped gland just in front of the windpipe. Causes include iodine deficiency, autoimmune thyroid disease (such as Graves' or Hashimoto's), nodules, or thyroid cancer.
甲状腺肿是甲状腺异常肿大，在颈部前方形成一个可见的肿块，吞咽时会上下移动。甲状腺是位于气管前方的小型蝴蝶形腺体。原因包括碘缺乏、自身免疫性甲状腺疾病（如格雷夫斯病或桥本甲状腺炎）、甲状腺结节或甲状腺癌。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what was the cause?
> 1) 何时确诊？原因是什么？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. thyroid function test/TFT, ultrasound, biopsy/HPE, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如甲状腺功能检查/TFT、超声波、活检/HPE、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was surgery or removal performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术或切除？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Were any HPE / biopsy tests done? If yes, state results.
> 4) 是否进行过病理/活检检查？如是，请说明结果。
> 5) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 5) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 6) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 6) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: thyroid function test（甲状腺功能检查）, ultrasound（超声波）, biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：甲状腺功能检查、超声波、活检/病理报告、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
> c) Thyroid Questionnaire completed by applicant
> c) 申请人填写甲状腺问卷
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> 于 dd/mm/yy 由 yy 医院 Dr xxx 医生诊断患有甲状腺肿。住院6天。
> Surgery performed to remove the enlarged lump. Blood and urine tests done on dd/mm/yy. Reports attached.
> 手术切除肿大腺体。dd/mm/yy 完成血液及尿液检查，报告随附。

> 需定期进行医疗随访。

## 核保结果

> Life / TPD / CI:
> 人寿 / 全残 / 严重疾病：
> - Depends on diagnosis type and investigation results
> - 视诊断类型及检查结果而定
>
> Medical:
> 医疗：
> - Standard if total thyroidectomy has been performed and HPE/biopsy confirms benign result
> - 已行全甲状腺切除术且病理/活检确认为良性者，标准承保
