#Medical_Underwriting/Endocrine_Diseases

Thyroid disease refers to conditions that affect the thyroid gland's ability to produce hormones in the right amounts. Hyperthyroidism means the thyroid overproduces hormones, causing the body to use energy too quickly. Hypothyroidism means it produces too little, slowing down body functions. Other thyroid conditions include nodules, goitre, and thyroid cancer. Most thyroid conditions are manageable with medication or surgery.
甲状腺疾病是指影响甲状腺正常分泌激素的各种病症。甲状腺功能亢进是甲状腺激素分泌过多，导致身体消耗能量过快；甲状腺功能减退是分泌过少，使身体机能减慢。其他甲状腺疾病包括甲状腺结节、甲状腺肿及甲状腺癌。大多数甲状腺疾病可通过药物或手术控制。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) When was it diagnosed, and what type (e.g. hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, nodule, goitre, cancer)?
> 1) 何时确诊？是哪种类型（如甲状腺功能减退、亢进、结节、甲状腺肿、癌症）？
> 2) Were any investigations done (e.g. thyroid function test/TFT, ultrasound, biopsy/HPE, blood/urine tests)? If yes, state dates and results.
> 2) 是否进行过检查（如甲状腺功能检查/TFT、超声波、活检/HPE、血液/尿液检查）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) Was any surgery or removal performed? If yes, state date and type.
> 3) 是否进行过手术或切除？如是，请说明日期及类型。
> 4) Any treatment or medication? If yes, what type and for how long?
> 4) 是否接受过治疗或服药？如是，说明类型及疗程。
> 5) Still under medical follow-up? If yes, how often?
> 5) 是否仍在定期随访？如是，多久一次？
> 6) Fully recovered or condition stable?
> 6) 是否已完全康复或病情稳定？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> From client / doctor:
> 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Latest reports: thyroid function test（甲状腺功能检查）, ultrasound（超声波）, biopsy/HPE（活检/病理报告）, blood/urine tests
> a) 最新报告：甲状腺功能检查、超声波、活检/病理报告、血液/尿液检查
> b) Surgery / removal report if performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术/切除报告
> c) Thyroid Questionnaire completed by applicant
> c) 申请人填写甲状腺问卷
>
> Medical examination / MAR / APS from treating doctor (where required)
> 体格检查 / 主诊医生报告（MAR）/ 主诊医生声明（APS）（视情况而定）
