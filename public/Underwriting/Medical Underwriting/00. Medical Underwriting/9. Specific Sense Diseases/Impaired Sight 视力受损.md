#Medical_Underwriting/Specific_Sense_Diseases

Means that a person's eyesight cannot be corrected to a "normal" level. Vision impairment may be caused by a loss of visual acuity, where the eye does not see objects as clearly as usual. Some people are completely blind, but many others have what's called legal blindness.
视力受损是指即使经过矫正，视力仍无法达到"正常"水平，可能由视觉敏锐度下降或其他眼部疾病引起。部分患者完全失明，但许多人属于法定失明的程度。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1. Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1. 实际诊断日期？
> 2. Any investigation tests such as vision, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2. 是否进行过任何检查（如视力、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3. 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4. 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1. From client / doctor:
> 1. 来自客户 / 医生：
> a) Any relevant reports such as vision test, blood & urine test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如视力测试、验血及尿液检查报告。
> b) Surgery report if has been performed
> b) 如有进行，附上手术报告
>
> 2. KIV – Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2. 备查 – 体格检查 / 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed to have left eye glaucoma on dd/mm/yy. Surgery has been performed on dd/mm/yy.
> 于 dd/mm/yy 患有左眼青光眼，手术已于 dd/mm/yy 进行。
> Normal vision acuity & intra-ocular pressure presently.
> 目前视力及眼压正常。
> On medical follow up currently.
> 目前在进行医疗随访。
