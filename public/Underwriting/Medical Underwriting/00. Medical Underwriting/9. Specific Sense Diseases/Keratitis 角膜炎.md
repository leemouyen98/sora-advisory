Inflammation of the cornea of the eye. The eye waters and is very painful and vision is blurred. It may be due to physical or chemical agents or result from infection.
角膜炎是眼睛角膜的炎症，症状包括流泪、眼痛剧烈及视力模糊，可由物理或化学因素引起，也可能源于感染。

Keratitis caused by fungi typically requires antifungal eye drops and oral antifungal medication. If a virus is causing the infection, antiviral eye drops and oral antiviral medications may be effective. Other viruses need only supportive care such as artificial tear drops.
真菌性角膜炎通常需要使用抗真菌眼药水及口服抗真菌药物；病毒性角膜炎可用抗病毒眼药水及口服抗病毒药物治疗，其他类型的病毒感染则只需支持性治疗，如使用人工泪液。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as vision, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如视力、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
