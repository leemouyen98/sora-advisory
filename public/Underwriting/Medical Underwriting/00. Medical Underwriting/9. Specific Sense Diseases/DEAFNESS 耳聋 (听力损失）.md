#Medical_Underwriting/Specific_Sense_Diseases

A person who is not able to hear as well as someone with normal hearing – hearing thresholds of 20 dB or better in both ears – is said to have hearing loss. Hearing loss may be mild, moderate, severe, or profound.
若某人的听力不如正常人——即双耳听力阈值达到20分贝或以上——则被称为有听力损失。听力损失可分为轻度、中度、重度或极重度。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as audiometry, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如听力测试、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS
## 所需文件

> 1) From client / doctor
> 1) 来自客户 / 医生
> a) Any relevant reports such as audiometry, blood & urine test?
> a) 任何相关报告，如听力测试、验血及尿液检查报告。
>
> 2) Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)
> 2) 医疗随行报告（MAR）/ 主治医生声明（APS）

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE
## 披露示例

> Diagnosed to have deafness of right ear at age of 6 due to ear infection.
> 因耳部感染，自6岁起右耳失聪。
> Normal hearing of left ear.
> 左耳听力正常。
