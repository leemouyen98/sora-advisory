Is a common eye problem that can make your vision blurry or distorted. It happens when your cornea (the clear front layer of your eye) or lens (an inner part of your eye that helps the eye focus) has a different shape than normal.
散光是常见的视力问题，会导致视物模糊或变形，原因是角膜（眼前透明层）或晶状体形状不规则。

Astigmatism is most commonly corrected with eyeglasses or contact lenses, but the only solution that can fix the underlying problem is refractive surgery, such as LASIK, PRK, the implanting of a Toric Lens Implant or performing Limbal Relaxing Incisions.
散光最常见的矫正方式是配戴眼镜或隐形眼镜，而唯一能根治根本问题的方法是屈光手术，如LASIK、PRK、植入复曲面人工晶状体或进行角膜缘松解切口。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as vision, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如视力、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
