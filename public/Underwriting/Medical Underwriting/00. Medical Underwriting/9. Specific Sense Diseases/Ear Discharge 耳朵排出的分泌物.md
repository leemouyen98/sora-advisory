#Medical_Underwriting/Specific_Sense_Diseases

Most of the time, any fluid leaking out of an ear is ear wax. A ruptured eardrum can cause a white, slightly bloody, or yellow discharge from the ear. Dry crusted material on a child's pillow is often a sign of a ruptured eardrum. The eardrum may also bleed.
大多数情况下，耳朵流出的液体是耳蜡。鼓膜穿孔可引起白色、略带血丝或黄色的耳道分泌物；儿童枕头上的干痂往往是鼓膜穿孔的迹象，鼓膜也可能出血。

Cloudy fluid or pus draining from the ear canal usually means there's an ear infection. The pus drains because there's a small tear in the eardrum.
耳道流出混浊液体或脓液，通常表示有耳部感染，脓液排出是因为鼓膜有小裂口。

Acute discharge in people without longstanding ear problems or a weakened immune system is usually not dangerous and is typically due to an external ear infection or a perforated eardrum resulting from a middle ear infection.
对于没有长期耳部问题或免疫力低下的人，急性耳道分泌物通常不危险，一般由外耳道感染或中耳炎导致鼓膜穿孔引起。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as audiometry, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如听力测试、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
