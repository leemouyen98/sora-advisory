#Medical_Underwriting/Specific_Sense_Diseases 

Is when you look at 1 object but can see 2 images. It may affect 1 eye or both eyes. Double vision isn’t something to ignore. While typically temporary, it may signal a serious problem, such as a brain aneurysm or stroke.

![[Double Vision 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1. Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2. Any investigation tests such vision,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1. From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / vision test ?
> b) Surgery report if has been performed
> 
> 2. KIV – Medical Examination / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Noticed to have double vision on dd/mm/yy.  currently on medication & on regular medical follow up. No surgery needed yet

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES

> Life / TPD/ CI / Medical insurance:
> – Postpone till fully investigated & firm diagnosis has been established
