Is a lack of vision.

Partial blindness means you have very limited vision. Complete blindness means you cannot see anything and do not see light. (Most people who use the term “blindness” mean complete blindness)

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1. Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2. Any investigation tests such vision,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1. From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / vision test ?
> 
> 2. Medical Examination

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have blindness both eye since age 7 due to accident.
> No medical follow up is required.
