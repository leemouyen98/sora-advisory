#Medical_Underwriting/Specific_Sense_Diseases

A condition in which loss of vision occurs because of an abnormally high pressure in the eye.
青光眼是因眼压异常升高导致视力丧失的状况。

It damages a nerve in the back of the eye called the optic nerve. The symptoms can start so slowly that you may not notice them. The only way to find out if you have glaucoma is to get a comprehensive dilated eye exam.
青光眼会损害眼睛后部称为视神经的神经，症状发展可能极为缓慢以至于无法察觉，只有通过全面的散瞳眼检才能确诊。

Surgery can't cure glaucoma or undo vision loss, but it can help protect your vision and stop it from getting worse.
手术无法治愈青光眼或恢复已失去的视力，但可帮助保护现有视力，防止进一步恶化。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1. Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1. 实际诊断日期？
> 2. Any investigation tests such as vision, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2. 是否进行过任何检查（如视力、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3. 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4. 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
