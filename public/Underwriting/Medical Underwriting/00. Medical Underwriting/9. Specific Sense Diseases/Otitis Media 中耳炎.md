Is an infection of the middle ear that causes inflammation (redness and swelling) and a build-up of fluid behind the eardrum. Anyone can develop a middle ear infection but infants between six and 15 months old are most commonly affected.
中耳炎是中耳感染，导致炎症（红肿）及鼓膜后方积液。任何人均可患中耳炎，但最常见于六至十五个月大的婴儿。

Most patients can be treated effectively with an analgesic such as a nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory medication or acetaminophen.
大多数患者可用止痛药（如非甾体类抗炎药或对乙酰氨基酚）有效治疗。

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
## 常见问题

> 1) Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 1) 实际诊断日期？
> 2) Any investigation tests such as audiometry, blood & urine tests etc.? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 2) 是否进行过任何检查（如听力测试、验血及尿液检查等）？如是，请说明日期及结果。
> 3) On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 3) 是否在服药/接受治疗？如是，说明治疗类型及疗程。
> 4) Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?
> 4) 是否仍在进行医疗随访？如是，多久一次（每周/每月等）？
