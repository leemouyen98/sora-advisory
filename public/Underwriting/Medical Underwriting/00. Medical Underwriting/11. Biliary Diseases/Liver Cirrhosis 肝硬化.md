#Medical_Underwriting/Biliary_Diseases

Is scarring (fibrosis) of the liver caused by long-term liver damage. The scar tissue prevents the liver working properly. Cirrhosis is sometimes called end-stage liver disease because it happens after other stages of damage from conditions that affect the liver, such as hepatitis.

Cirrhosis is a type of liver damage where healthy cells are replaced by scar tissue. Common causes include excessive drinking of alcohol, hepatitis B and C virus infections, and fatty liver that’s caused by obesity and diabetes.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1)  Actual & date diagnosis ?
> 2)  Any investigation tests such endoscopy,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1)  From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood (Liver function Test) / urine / scan test ?
> 
> 2)  Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have liver cirrhosis on dd/mm/yy. Regularly follow up by Dr xxx of yy hospital.
