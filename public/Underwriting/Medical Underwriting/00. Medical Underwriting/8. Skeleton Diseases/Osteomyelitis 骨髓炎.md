Is inflammation or swelling that occurs in the bone. It can result from an infection somewhere else in the body that has spread to the bone, or it can start in the bone; often as a result of an injury. 

Osteomyelitis is a serious condition that requires immediate treatment. Most bone infections go away when you take antibiotics. Be sure to take all your prescribed medication even if you start feeling better.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1. Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 2. Any investigation tests such CT / MRI scanning, X-ray,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1. From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / CT / MRI scan test ?
>
> 2. Medical Examination  / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have osteomyelitis on dd/mm/yy. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital,  
> On medication & regular medical follow up currently.
