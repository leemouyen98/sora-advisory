#Medical_Underwriting/Skeleton_Diseases

Compression of the median nerve as it passes through the wrist. This causes pain and numbness in the index and middle fingers and weakness of the thumb.

Surgery for carpal tunnel syndrome is usually done as an outpatient. Two types of carpal tunnel surgery are done: open surgery and endoscopic surgery. You may have local or general anesthesia, or both, for either surgery.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1. Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 2. Any investigation tests such CT / MRI scanning, X-ray,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3. On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4. Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1. From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / CT / MRI scan test ?
>
> 2. Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have carpal tunnel syndrome on dd/mm/yy. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital & surgery has been performed,  
> Fully recovered with full range of movement currently, no medical follow up is required.
