Is a break in the continuity of a bone. A significant percentage of bone fractures occur because of high force impact or stress. However, a fracture may also be the result of some medical conditions that weaken the bones. These include osteoporosis and some types of cancer.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> Any investigation tests such CT / MRI scanning, X-ray,  blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1. From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine / CT / MRI scan test ?
>
> 2. Medical Examination
>
> 3. KIV – Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have left humerus fracture due to accident 12 years ago. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital & surgery with internal fixation has been performed.  as told removal was not required
> fully recovered with no limping/gait of left leg.
