#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease 

Is a sexually transmitted disease (STD) caused by a herpes simplex virus (HSV). It can cause sores on your genital or rectal area, buttocks, and thighs. You can get it from having vaginal, anal, or oral sex with someone who has it.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1)  Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 2)  Any investigation tests such blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results. 
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1)  From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine test ?
> 
> 2) KIV – Medical Examination  / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Told to have genital herpes after a test confirmation on dd/mm/yy. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital, 
> On medication & medical follow up currently.

## POSSIBILITIES OF UNDERWRITING OUTCOMES

> Life / TPD / CI / Medical insurance:
> – Postpone till fully investigated & well treated
