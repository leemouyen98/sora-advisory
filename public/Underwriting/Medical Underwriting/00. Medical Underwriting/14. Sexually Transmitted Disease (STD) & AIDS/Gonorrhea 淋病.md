#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease

Is an infection caused by a sexually transmitted bacterium that infects both males and females. Gonorrhea most often affects the urethra, rectum or throat. In females, gonorrhea can also infect the cervix. Gonorrhea is most commonly spread during vaginal, oral or anal sex.

![[Gonorrhea 01.png]]

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1)  Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 2)  Any investigation tests such blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1)  From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine test ?
>
> 2) KIV – Medical Examination  / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have gonorrhea on dd/mm/yy. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital,
> On medication & regular medical follow up currently.
