#Medical_Underwriting/Sexually_Transmitted_Disease

Is a term for the symptoms and illness caused by infection with the human immunodeficiency virus (HIV). HIV infection, however, takes a very long time to develop into full blown AIDS. The virus begins to replicate in the body within the CD4 cells and begins to destroy the immunity.

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> 1)  Actual & date of diagnosis ?
> 2)  Any investigation tests such blood & urine tests etc… ? If yes, please state the dates & results.
> 3)  On medication / treatment ? If yes, what type & period of treatment.
> 4)  Still on medical follow up ? If yes, how frequent (weekly / monthly etc) ?

## COMMON REQUIREMENTS

> 1)  From client / doctor
> a)  Any relevant reports such as blood / urine test ?
>
> 2) KIV – Medical Examination  / Medical attendant report (MAR) / Attending physician statement (APS)

## EXAMPLE OF DISCLOSURE

> Diagnosed to have AIDS after blood test confirmation on dd/mm/yy. Consulted  Dr xxx of yy hospital,
> No medication & medical follow up currently.
