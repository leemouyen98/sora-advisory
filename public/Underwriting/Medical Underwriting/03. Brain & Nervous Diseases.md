Have you ever had, been diagnosed to have, been investigated or treated for or advised to seek any medical or surgical treatment for any of the conditions listed below :

Stroke, transient ischemic attack (TIA), brain hemorrhage or brain injury, epilepsy, convulsion (fits), Paralysis, Parkinson’s disease, Alzheimer’s disease, multiple sclerosis, prolonged recurrent dizziness or headache, migraine, cerebral palsy or other disease or disorder of the brain or nervous system ?

# THE BRAIN 脑部
The brain is a complex organ that controls thought, memory, emotion, touch, motor skills, vision, breathing, temperature, hunger and every process that regulates our body.

The average adult human brain weighs about 1400g (approximately 2% of total body weight), and has 3 structures; cerebrum, brain stem and cerebellum.
